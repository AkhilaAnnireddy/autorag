Disaster Recovery Plan for NovaTech Inc.

Introduction
The purpose of this Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP) is to ensure the continuity of NovaTech Inc.'s business operations in the event of a disaster. The plan outlines the procedures to be followed in the event of a disaster, including the restoration of critical systems and the recovery of data.

Policies and Procedures
All employees are required to follow the procedures outlined in this plan. Failure to comply with the plan may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination. The plan will be reviewed and updated in accordance with the NovaTech Inc. IT Policy, which is reviewed and updated annually.
