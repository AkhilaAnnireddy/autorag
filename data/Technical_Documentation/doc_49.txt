Disaster Recovery Plan for NovaTech Inc.

Introduction
The purpose of this disaster recovery plan is to ensure the continuity of NovaTech Inc.'s business operations in the event of a disaster. This plan outlines the procedures to be followed in the event of a disaster, including the restoration of critical systems and the recovery of data. The plan is based on the assumption that a disaster may occur at any time, and that it is essential to be prepared to respond quickly and effectively.
