
   <h1>Intern Policy</h1>
   <p>Welcome to XYZ Corporation, a leading technology firm. Our intern policy is designed to provide a comprehensive and structured program for interns to learn and grow with our organization. The internship program is open to students and recent graduates who are looking to gain hands-on experience in their field of study.</p>
   <h2>Eligibility Criteria</h2>
   <p>To be eligible for the internship program, applicants must meet the following criteria:</p>
   <table>
      <tr>
         <th>Criteria</th>
         <th>Requirements</th>
      </tr>
      <tr>
         <td>Age</td>
         <td>18 years or older</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
         <td>Education</td>
         <td>Currently enrolled in a degree-granting program or recently graduated</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
         <td>Availability</td>
         <td>Available to work for a minimum of 10 weeks</td>
      </tr>
   </table>
   <h2>Application Process</h2>
