
   Our Code of Conduct is a critical component of our company's culture and values. It outlines the expectations for all employees, officers, and directors of GreenTech Inc. in terms of their behavior and decision-making. The Code is reviewed and updated annually by the Compliance Committee, which is composed of representatives from various departments, including HR, Legal, and Finance.

   * Purpose:
     * To promote a culture of integrity, respect, and accountability
     * To ensure compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies
     * To provide guidance on ethical behavior and decision-making
   * Scope:
     * This Code applies to all employees, officers, and directors of GreenTech Inc.
     * It also applies to contractors, vendors, and third-party representatives who work on behalf of the company
   * Responsibilities:
     * All employees are responsible for adhering to the Code and reporting any violations or concerns
     * Managers and supervisors are responsible for enforcing the Code and providing guidance and support to their team members
     * The Compliance Committee is responsible for reviewing and updating the Code, as well as investigating any reported violations

   The following are some key policies and procedures that are outlined in the Code:
   * Anti-Discrimination and Harassment Policy:
     * GreenTech Inc. is committed to providing a workplace that is free from discrimination and harassment
     * All employees are expected to treat each other with respect and dignity, regardless of their race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or any other protected characteristic
     * Any form of discrimination or harassment will not be tolerated and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination
   * Confidentiality and Data Protection Policy:
     * All employees are expected to maintain the confidentiality of company information and data
     * This includes customer information, financial data, and other sensitive information
     * Employees are expected to follow all applicable laws and regulations, as well as company policies and procedures, when handling confidential information
   * Reporting Violations:
     * Employees are encouraged to report any violations of the Code or any other company policies
     * Reports can be made to a manager, supervisor, or HR representative
     * All reports will be investigated promptly and thoroughly, and any necessary actions will be taken

   GreenTech Inc. takes a proactive approach to preventing and detecting violations of the Code. This includes:
   * Regular audits and monitoring to ensure compliance with company policies and applicable laws and regulations
   * A whistleblower policy that allows employees to report any concerns or violations anonymously
   * A system for tracking and investigating reports of violations, as well as taking disciplinary action when necessary
   * The company also has a zero-tolerance policy for retaliation against employees who report violations or concerns in good faith.

   * Auditing and Monitoring:
     * The company conducts regular audits and monitoring to ensure compliance with company policies and applicable laws and regulations
     * This includes reviewing employee behavior, company records, and other relevant information
     * Any violations or concerns that are identified during the auditing and monitoring process will be investigated and addressed promptly
   * Whistleblower Policy:
     * The company has a whistleblower policy that allows employees to report any concerns or violations anonymously
     * The policy is designed to protect employees from retaliation and to ensure that all reports are investigated promptly and thoroughly
     * Employees can report concerns or violations to a manager, supervisor, or HR representative, or through the company's anonymous reporting system

   The company's Code of Conduct is based on the following principles:
   * Respect for the law and applicable regulations
   * Respect for colleagues, customers, and other stakeholders
   * Honesty and integrity in all interactions and transactions
   * Accountability for one's actions and decisions
   * Transparency and openness in all dealings

   * Principles in Practice:
     * The company expects all employees to adhere to these principles in their daily work and interactions
     * This includes being honest and transparent in all communications, treating colleagues and customers with respect, and taking responsibility for one's actions and decisions
     * The company also expects employees to report any violations or concerns that they become aware of, and to cooperate fully with any investigations or audits
