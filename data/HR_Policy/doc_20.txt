Introduction to Whistleblower Policy

At NovaTech Inc., we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and ethics in our business practices. As part of this commitment, we have established a whistleblower policy to provide a safe and confidential way for employees to report any concerns or wrongdoing. This policy applies to all employees, officers, and directors of NovaTech Inc. and its subsidiaries.

### Purpose and Scope
The purpose of this policy is to encourage employees to report any unethical or illegal behavior, and to protect them from retaliation for making such reports. The scope of this policy includes, but is not limited to, reports of financial impropriety, harassment, discrimination, and other forms of misconduct.

In 2020, our company reported a total of 25 incidents of misconduct, with 15 of these incidents being reported through our whistleblower hotline. We take all reports seriously and have implemented a thorough investigation process to ensure that all incidents are properly addressed. Our Chief Compliance Officer, Jane Smith, is responsible for overseeing the implementation of this policy and ensuring that all reports are handled in a fair and timely manner.

### Reporting Procedures
Employees who wish to report a concern or wrongdoing can do so by contacting our whistleblower hotline at 1-800-NOVA-TECH (1-800-668-2832) or by submitting a report online through our website. All reports will be treated as confidential and will be investigated promptly. Employees can also report concerns to their supervisor or HR representative, but they are not required to do so.

Here are the steps to follow when reporting a concern:

* Step 1: Gather all relevant information and documentation related to the concern
* Step 2: Contact the whistleblower hotline or submit a report online
* Step 3: Cooperate fully with the investigation
* Step 4: Follow up with the investigator to ensure that the matter is being addressed

We have a dedicated team of investigators who are trained to handle all types of reports. Our investigators will review each report and determine the appropriate course of action. In some cases, we may need to involve external authorities, such as law enforcement or regulatory agencies.

### Protection from Retaliation
NovaTech Inc. is committed to protecting employees from retaliation for reporting concerns or wrongdoing. Retaliation against an employee who reports a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination. Our policy is to ensure that all employees feel safe and supported when reporting concerns.

Here are some examples of retaliation:

1. Demotion or termination
2. Reduction in pay or benefits
3. Discrimination or harassment
4. Intimidation or threats

If an employee experiences any form of retaliation, they should immediately report it to our Chief Compliance Officer or HR representative. We take all reports of retaliation seriously and will investigate them promptly.

### Confidentiality
All reports will be treated as confidential to the extent possible. However, in some cases, we may need to disclose the identity of the reporting employee to conduct a thorough investigation. In such cases, we will notify the employee and obtain their consent before disclosing their identity.

We have implemented a number of measures to ensure confidentiality, including:

* Secure online reporting system
* Confidential hotline
* Locked and secure files
* Limited access to investigation files

### Investigation Procedure
All reports will be investigated promptly and thoroughly. Our investigators will gather all relevant information and documentation, and will interview witnesses and parties involved. We will also review all relevant policies and procedures to determine if they were followed.

Here is a table outlining our investigation procedure:

| Step | Description | Timeline |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Initial review of report | Within 24 hours |
| 2 | Assignment of investigator | Within 48 hours |
| 3 | Gathering of evidence | Ongoing |
| 4 | Interview with reporting employee | Within 1 week |
| 5 | Conclusion of investigation | Within 30 days |

Our investigators will provide regular updates to the reporting employee and will notify them of the outcome of the investigation. If the investigation finds that misconduct has occurred, we will take prompt and appropriate action to address the issue.

### Conclusion
In conclusion, our whistleblower policy is an important part of our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and ethics. We encourage all employees to report any concerns or wrongdoing, and we are committed to protecting them from retaliation. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please do not hesitate to contact our Chief Compliance Officer or HR representative.
