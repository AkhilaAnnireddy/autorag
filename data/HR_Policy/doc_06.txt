
      # Introduction to Expense Reimbursement Policy
      The Expense Reimbursement Policy is designed to provide guidelines for employees of NovaTech Inc. regarding the reimbursement of expenses incurred during the course of their employment. This policy is effective as of January 1, 2023, and supersedes all prior policies. 
      
      ## Purpose and Scope
      The purpose of this policy is to ensure that employees are reimbursed for legitimate business expenses in a fair and timely manner. This policy applies to all employees of NovaTech Inc., including full-time, part-time, and temporary employees. 
      
      ## Definitions
      For the purpose of this policy, the following definitions shall apply: 
      - Business Expense: Any expense incurred by an employee for a legitimate business purpose.
      - Reimbursable Expense: A business expense that is eligible for reimbursement under this policy.
      
      # Eligible Expenses
      ## Transportation Expenses
      Employees are eligible for reimbursement of transportation expenses incurred while traveling on company business. This includes airfare, rental cars, and mileage driven in a personal vehicle for business purposes. The mileage reimbursement rate is 58 cents per mile, as per the IRS guidelines for 2023. 
      
      ## Meal Expenses
      Meal expenses are reimbursable up to a maximum of $50 per day for domestic travel and $75 per day for international travel. Receipts are required for all meal expenses. 
      
      ## Lodging Expenses
      Lodging expenses are reimbursable up to a maximum of $200 per night for domestic travel and $300 per night for international travel. Receipts are required for all lodging expenses. 
      
      # Ineligible Expenses
      ## Personal Expenses
      Personal expenses, such as entertainment and gifts, are not eligible for reimbursement under this policy. 
      
      ## Expenses Not Supported by Receipts
      Expenses that are not supported by receipts or other documentation are not eligible for reimbursement. 
      
      # Reimbursement Procedure
      ## Submission of Expenses
      Employees must submit their expenses for reimbursement within 30 days of incurring the expense. Expenses must be submitted using the company's online expense reporting system. 
      
      ## Approval of Expenses
      All expenses must be approved by the employee's supervisor before reimbursement can be made. 
      
      # Payment of Reimbursements
      Reimbursements will be paid to employees on a bi-weekly basis. The payment date will be the 15th and 30th of each month. 
      
      # Responsibilities
      ## Employee Responsibilities
      Employees are responsible for ensuring that all expenses submitted for reimbursement are legitimate business expenses and are supported by receipts and other documentation. 
      
      ## Supervisor Responsibilities
      Supervisors are responsible for reviewing and approving all expenses submitted by their employees. 
      
      # Policy Administration
      This policy will be administered by the Human Resources Department. Any questions or concerns regarding this policy should be directed to the Human Resources Department. 
      
      # Amendments to the Policy
      This policy may be amended or modified at any time by NovaTech Inc. without notice. 
      
      # Acknowledgement
      By submitting expenses for reimbursement, employees acknowledge that they have read, understand, and will comply with this policy.
