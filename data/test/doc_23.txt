
   <h1>Code of Conduct</h1>
   <p>Welcome to XYZ Corporation, a leading provider of innovative solutions. Our Code of Conduct outlines the expectations for all employees, officers, and directors. It is the responsibility of each individual to adhere to this code and report any violations to their supervisor or HR representative. The code is reviewed and updated annually, with the most recent revision dated January 10, 2023.</p>
   <h2>Introduction</h2>
   <p>At XYZ Corporation, we value integrity, respect, and fairness. We strive to maintain a work environment that is free from discrimination, harassment, and retaliation. Our goal is to provide a safe and healthy workplace for all employees. The following tables outline our policies and procedures:</p>
   <table>
      <tr>
         <th>Policy</th>
         <th>Description</th>
      </tr>
      <tr>
         <td>Anti-Discrimination Policy</td>
         <td>XZY Corporation is committed to providing a work environment that is free from discrimination based on race, color, national origin, sex, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
         <td>Harassment Policy</td>
         <td>XZY Corporation prohibits all forms of harassment, including sexual harassment, and will take prompt and effective action to investigate and address any complaints.</td>
      </tr>
   </table>

   <table>

         <th>Access Restrictions</th>
      </tr>

         <td>Authorized personnel only, with two-factor authentication</td>

      <tr>

   <table>
      <tr>

      </tr>
      <tr>

      </tr>
