Q3 Quarterly Earnings Report for NovaTech Inc.

Introduction:
NovaTech Inc. is pleased to report its Q3 quarterly earnings for the period ending September 30, 2022. This report highlights the company's financial performance and provides an overview of its operations during the quarter.

Table 1: Q3 Financial Highlights
| Category | Q3 2022 | Q3 2021 | % Change
| --- | --- | --- | ---
| Revenue | $1,234,567 | $1,012,345 | 21.8%
| Net Income | $123,456 | $101,234 | 21.9%
| Earnings Per Share (EPS) | $1.23 | $1.01 | 21.8%

Operating Results:
The company's revenue for Q3 2022 was $1,234,567, representing a 21.8% increase from $1,012,345 in Q3 2021. This growth was driven by a 25% increase in sales from our software division, led by our CEO, Emily J. Smith. The net income for the quarter was $123,456, up 21.9% from $101,234 in the same period last year.
