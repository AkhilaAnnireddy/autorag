This Vendor Contract Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into on February 10, 2024 ('Effective Date') by and between GreenTech Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ('GreenTech'), and NovaSpire LLC, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York, with its principal place of business at 456 Broadway, New York, NY 10013 ('NovaSpire'). The parties agree to the terms and conditions outlined below. GreenTech is engaged in the business of developing and marketing innovative software solutions, and NovaSpire is a leading provider of cloud infrastructure services. The parties wish to establish a strategic partnership whereby NovaSpire will provide cloud infrastructure services to support GreenTech's software development and deployment. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of three (3) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of Section 10. The following are the key terms of the partnership: 
   
 Table 1: Partnership Terms 
   | Term | Description | 
   | --- | --- | 
   | Term Length | Three (3) years | 
   | Renewal | Automatic, unless terminated by either party with thirty (30) days' written notice | 
   | Termination | By either party, upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the other | 
   The partnership will be governed by the following policies: 
   
 
   1. Data Security Policy: NovaSpire will comply with all applicable data security laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation ('GDPR') and the California Consumer Privacy Act ('CCPA'). 
   2. Intellectual Property Policy: GreenTech retains all rights, title, and interest in and to its software solutions, and NovaSpire retains all rights, title, and interest in and to its cloud infrastructure services. 
   3. Service Level Agreement ('SLA') Policy: NovaSpire will provide cloud infrastructure services in accordance with the SLA attached as Exhibit A. 
   The following are the payment terms: 
   
 
   * Payment Amount: GreenTech will pay NovaSpire an annual fee of $250,000, payable in quarterly installments of $62,500. 
   * Payment Due Date: The payment is due on the first day of each quarter. 
   * Late Payment Fee: A late payment fee of 1.5% per month will be charged on any overdue payments. 
   In the event of a dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, the parties agree to negotiate in good faith to resolve the dispute. If the dispute cannot be resolved through negotiation, it will be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitration will be conducted by a single arbitrator, and the decision of the arbitrator will be final and binding on the parties. 
   
 
   Table 2: Dispute Resolution Process 
   | Step | Description |

   | Step 2 | Arbitration: If the dispute cannot be resolved through negotiation, it will be resolved through arbitration. | 
   | Step 3 | Final Decision: The decision of the arbitrator will be final and binding on the parties. |
