This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement"), dated as of February 10, 2023 (the "Effective Date"), is entered into by and between NovaTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001 ("NovaTech"), and Apex Solutions, a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Apex"), collectively referred to as the "Parties". The Parties are entering into this Agreement to facilitate the exchange of confidential and proprietary information in connection with a potential business relationship between NovaTech and Apex. As of the Effective Date, NovaTech and Apex wish to establish a mutually beneficial relationship, with NovaTech providing certain technical data and trade secrets to Apex, and Apex providing certain marketing and sales expertise to NovaTech. The exchange of such information is contingent upon the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of two (2) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement, all confidential information disclosed by NovaTech to Apex shall be returned to NovaTech, and Apex shall certify in writing that all such confidential information has been destroyed. The Parties agree that the confidential information disclosed by NovaTech to Apex shall include, but not be limited to, certain technical data and trade secrets related to NovaTech's proprietary software, including the source code and object code for such software. Such confidential information shall be disclosed to Apex solely for the purpose of evaluating the potential business relationship between NovaTech and Apex, and shall not be used for any other purpose.
