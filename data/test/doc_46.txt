
   Data Privacy Policy of GreenTech Inc.
   Effective Date: January 1, 2022

   Introduction:
   * This Data Privacy Policy outlines the principles and practices that GreenTech Inc. follows in the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.
   * GreenTech Inc. is committed to protecting the personal data of its customers, employees, and partners.
   * This policy applies to all personal data collected, used, or disclosed by GreenTech Inc., regardless of the media or format.

   Collection of Personal Data:
   * GreenTech Inc. collects personal data from various sources, including:
     * Customer registration forms
     * Employee application forms
     * Partner agreements
   * The types of personal data collected include:
     * Name and contact information
     * Demographic information
     * Financial information
   * GreenTech Inc. collects personal data for the following purposes:
     * To provide products and services to customers
     * To manage employee relationships
     * To facilitate business partnerships

   Use of Personal Data:
   * GreenTech Inc. uses personal data for the purposes for which it was collected.
   * GreenTech Inc. may also use personal data for secondary purposes, such as:
     * Marketing and advertising
     * Research and development
     * Compliance with laws and regulations
   * GreenTech Inc. will obtain consent from individuals before using their personal data for secondary purposes.

   Disclosure of Personal Data:
   * GreenTech Inc. discloses personal data to third parties in the following circumstances:
     * To provide products and services to customers
     * To manage employee relationships
     * To facilitate business partnerships
   * GreenTech Inc. will ensure that third parties to whom personal data is disclosed have adequate safeguards in place to protect the data.
   * GreenTech Inc. will obtain consent from individuals before disclosing their personal data to third parties.

   Security and Protection of Personal Data:
   * GreenTech Inc. has implemented reasonable security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
   * GreenTech Inc. uses encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect personal data.
   * GreenTech Inc. will notify individuals in the event of a data breach.

   Retention of Personal Data:
   * GreenTech Inc. will retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.
   * GreenTech Inc. will dispose of personal data in a secure manner when it is no longer needed.
   * The retention period for personal data is as follows:
     * Customer data: 5 years
     * Employee data: 7 years
     * Partner data: 10 years

   Access and Correction of Personal Data:
   * Individuals have the right to access and correct their personal data.
   * GreenTech Inc. will provide individuals with access to their personal data within 30 days of receipt of a request.
   * GreenTech Inc. will correct personal data that is inaccurate or incomplete.

   Changes to this Policy:
   * GreenTech Inc. reserves the right to modify or update this Data Privacy Policy at any time.
   * Changes to this policy will be effective immediately.
   * GreenTech Inc. will notify individuals of changes to this policy by posting a notice on its website.
