The user research findings report for the General Business domain was conducted over a period of six months, from January 2022 to June 2022. The research was led by Dr. Emily Chen, a renowned expert in the field of business research, and her team of five researchers. The research aimed to identify the key challenges faced by small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the current market. According to the research, 75% of SMEs reported that their biggest challenge was accessing funding, while 60% reported that they struggled with marketing and advertising. The research also found that 80% of SMEs believed that the current economic climate was uncertain and that this uncertainty was affecting their ability to make long-term plans. The team conducted interviews with 50 SME owners and managers, and the data collected was analyzed using thematic analysis. The research was funded by the National Business Association, a non-profit organization that aims to support SMEs.
