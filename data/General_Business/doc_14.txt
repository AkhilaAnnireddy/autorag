Business Continuity Plan for XYZ Corporation

Introduction

The purpose of this Business Continuity Plan (BCP) is to ensure the continuation of business operations in the event of a disaster or major disruption. This plan outlines the procedures and guidelines for responding to and recovering from such events. The BCP will be reviewed and updated annually, or as needed.

Appendix B: Recovery Procedures

The following procedures will be followed in the event of a disaster or major disruption:

1. Activate the emergency response team
2. Assess the damage and impact on the business
3. Implement the recovery strategies outlined in the plan
4. Communicate with employees, customers, and stakeholders
5. Review and update the plan as needed
