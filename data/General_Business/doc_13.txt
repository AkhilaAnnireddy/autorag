
   Risk Management Plan for Azure Enterprises
   The risk management plan for Azure Enterprises is designed to identify, assess, and mitigate potential risks that could impact the company's operations, financial performance, and reputation. The plan is developed in accordance with the company's risk management policy, which is reviewed and updated annually.

   * Risk Management Policy:
     * The risk management policy is based on the following principles:
       * Proactive approach to risk management
       * Continuous monitoring and review of risks
       * Collaboration and communication among departments and stakeholders
       * Compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards
     * The policy is approved by the Board of Directors and is communicated to all employees.

   The risk management process involves the following steps:
   * Risk Identification:
     * Identify potential risks through brainstorming sessions, reviews of financial statements, and analysis of industry trends
     * Categorize risks into strategic, operational, financial, and compliance risks
     * Prioritize risks based on likelihood and potential impact
   * Risk Assessment:
     * Assess the likelihood and potential impact of each risk
     * Evaluate the effectiveness of existing controls and mitigation measures
     * Identify gaps in controls and mitigation measures
   * Risk Mitigation:
     * Develop and implement mitigation measures to reduce the likelihood and potential impact of each risk
     * Assign responsibility for implementing mitigation measures to specific departments and employees
     * Establish timelines for implementing mitigation measures

   * Compliance Risks:
     * Risk of non-compliance with regulatory requirements: 10% likelihood, $500,000 potential impact
     * Risk of non-compliance with industry standards: 5% likelihood, $200,000 potential impact

   The following mitigation measures have been developed and implemented:
   * Strategic Mitigation Measures:
     * Diversification of products and services
     * Expansion into new markets
     * Development of strategic partnerships
   * Operational Mitigation Measures:
     * Implementation of supply chain management system
     * Development of disaster recovery plan
     * Provision of training and development programs for employees
   * Financial Mitigation Measures:
     * Maintenance of cash reserves
     * Hedging against currency fluctuations
     * Monitoring of interest rates and adjustment of financing strategies
   * Compliance Mitigation Measures:
     * Establishment of compliance department
     * Development of compliance training programs
     * Regular audits and reviews of compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards
