
      # Introduction to Brand Guidelines
      Our brand guidelines are designed to ensure consistency across all platforms and media. At NovaTech Inc., we take pride in our brand and strive to maintain its integrity. These guidelines were last updated on February 10, 2023, and are applicable to all employees, partners, and vendors.

      ## Brand Overview
      NovaTech Inc. was founded in 2005 by CEO, Emily Chen, with the mission to provide innovative technology solutions. Our brand is built on the values of innovation, customer satisfaction, and teamwork. We have grown significantly over the years, with over 500 employees worldwide and offices in New York, London, and Tokyo.

      # Visual Identity
      ## Logo
      The NovaTech Inc. logo is a combination of a blue circle and a white letter 'N'. The blue color represents trust and stability, while the white color represents clarity and innovation. The logo should always be used in its entirety, with a minimum size of 1 inch in diameter.

      ## Color Palette
      Our primary color is Nova Blue (#4567b7), and our secondary color is Nova White (#ffffff). The color palette should be used consistently across all platforms, including our website, social media, and marketing materials.

      # Typography
      Our primary font is Open Sans, and our secondary font is Arial. Font sizes and styles should be used consistently, with headings in font size 18-24 and body text in font size 12-16.

      # Tone of Voice
      Our tone of voice is professional, friendly, and approachable. We strive to communicate clearly and concisely, avoiding jargon and technical terms whenever possible.

      # Social Media
      We have a strong presence on social media platforms, including Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Our social media accounts are managed by the marketing team, and all posts must be approved by the marketing manager.

      # Compliance
      All employees, partners, and vendors must comply with our brand guidelines. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of contract or employment.

      # Contact Information
      For any questions or concerns regarding our brand guidelines, please contact the marketing team at marketing@novatech.com. We are available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST.
