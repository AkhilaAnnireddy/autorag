Our company, NovaTech Inc., was founded in 2005 by CEO, John Lee. As of 2022, we have over 500 employees across three locations: New York, Chicago, and San Francisco. Our mission is to provide innovative solutions to the tech industry. We strive to maintain a positive and productive work environment, and our employee benefits and policies are designed to support this goal.

Table 3: Employee Conduct and Behavior
| Policy | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Code of Conduct | Maintain a professional and respectful attitude |
| Confidentiality Agreement | Protect company and client information |
| Social Media Policy | Avoid posting sensitive or confidential information |

Our employee conduct and behavior policies are in place to ensure a safe and respectful work environment. All employees are expected to adhere to these policies, and any violations will be addressed promptly. Our HR department, led by Director, Emily Chen, is responsible for implementing and enforcing these policies.

Table 4: Performance Evaluation and Development
| Evaluation | Description | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Annual Review | Comprehensive performance evaluation | Once a year |
| Quarterly Check-in | Progress update and goal setting | Every 3 months |
| Training and Development | Opportunities for skill improvement | As needed |

Our performance evaluation and development process is designed to support employee growth and development. Employees are expected to participate in regular evaluations and training sessions to improve their skills and performance. Our management team, including Department Managers, Michael Kim and Sarah Taylor, work closely with employees to set goals and provide feedback.

Table 5: Employee Recognition and Rewards
| Program | Description | Eligibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Employee of the Month | Recognition and reward for outstanding performance | All employees |
| Quarterly Bonus | Monetary reward for meeting sales targets | Sales team |
| Annual Awards | Recognition and reward for exceptional contributions | All employees |

We believe in recognizing and rewarding our employees for their hard work and contributions. Our employee recognition and rewards programs are designed to motivate and inspire employees to excel in their roles. Our CEO, John Lee, presents the annual awards to deserving employees at our company-wide meeting in December.

As of January 1, 2023, our company will be implementing a new policy requiring all employees to complete a mandatory training program on diversity and inclusion. This program will be led by our HR department and will be completed within the first 6 months of employment. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment, and this program is an important step towards achieving this goal.

For more information on our employee benefits and policies, please contact our HR department at hr@novatech.com. We are always happy to answer any questions and provide support to our employees.
