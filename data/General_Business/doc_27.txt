Our customer journey map is divided into five stages: awareness, consideration, purchase, retention, and advocacy. At the awareness stage, potential customers are introduced to our brand through various marketing channels, including social media, search engines, and online advertisements. We have allocated a budget of $500,000 for our marketing efforts in 2022, with a focus on increasing our online presence and reach. Once customers have become aware of our brand, they enter the consideration stage, where they evaluate our services and compare them to those of our competitors. At this stage, customers are looking for detailed information about our services, including pricing, features, and customer support. We have found that customers who engage with our content, such as blog posts and videos, are more likely to proceed to the purchase stage. In fact, according to our data, customers who watch at least one video on our website are 30% more likely to make a purchase. The purchase stage is a critical point in the customer journey, as it is the moment when customers decide to commit to our services. To facilitate a smooth purchase experience, we offer a range of payment options, including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. After a customer has made a purchase, they enter the retention stage, where we aim to build a long-term relationship with them. We achieve this through regular communication, including newsletters, promotional offers, and surveys. Our customer retention rate is currently at 60%, which means that 60% of our customers continue to use our services after their initial purchase. Finally, the advocacy stage is where our loyal customers become ambassadors for our brand, recommending our services to friends, family, and colleagues. We encourage advocacy through our referral program, which offers rewards and incentives for successful referrals. As of January 2022, our referral program has generated over 1,000 new leads, resulting in a significant increase in sales.
