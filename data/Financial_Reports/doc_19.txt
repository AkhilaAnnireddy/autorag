Auditor Findings Report for Fiscal Year 2022

Introduction:
Our audit of XYZ Corporation's financial statements for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2022, was conducted in accordance with auditing standards generally accepted in the United States of America. The audit was performed by our team, led by Senior Auditor, Jane Doe.

Table 1: Financial Highlights
| Category | 2022 | 2021 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Revenue | $10,000,000 | $8,000,000 |
| Net Income | $2,000,000 | $1,500,000 |
| Total Assets | $50,000,000 | $40,000,000 |

Audit Findings:
Our audit revealed several findings that are presented in the following tables.

Table 2: Material Weaknesses
| Weakness | Description | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Inadequate Internal Controls | Lack of segregation of duties in the accounting department | Implement additional controls to ensure proper segregation of duties |
| Inaccurate Financial Reporting | Incorrect accounting for revenue recognition | Review and revise financial reporting policies to ensure compliance with GAAP |

Table 3: Significant Deficiencies
| Deficiency | Description | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Insufficient Audit Evidence | Inadequate documentation of audit procedures | Maintain detailed records of audit procedures and evidence |
| Untimely Financial Reporting | Delayed submission of financial statements | Establish a timeline for financial reporting to ensure timely submission |

Conclusion:
In conclusion, our audit of XYZ Corporation's financial statements for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2022, identified several areas for improvement. We recommend that management implement the recommendations presented in this report to address the material weaknesses and significant deficiencies identified during the audit.
