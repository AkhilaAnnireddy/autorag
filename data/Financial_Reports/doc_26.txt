
   # Introduction to Payroll Expense Report
   The payroll expense report for the first quarter of 2023 is presented below. This report covers the period from January 1, 2023, to March 31, 2023. It includes all payroll expenses incurred by the company during this time.

   ## Overview of Payroll Expenses
   The total payroll expense for the first quarter of 2023 was $1,234,567. This amount includes salaries, wages, benefits, and taxes for all employees. The breakdown of these expenses is as follows:
   - Salaries: $750,000

   - Benefits: $150,000

   ### Benefits Expenses

   - Health insurance: $75,000
   - Retirement plans: $40,000
   - Other benefits: $35,000

   ## Tax Expenses
   Tax expenses for the first quarter of 2023 totaled $134,567. This amount includes federal, state, and local taxes paid on behalf of all employees. The breakdown of these expenses is as follows:
   - Federal taxes: $67,000
   - State taxes: $30,000
   - Local taxes: $37,567

   # Glossary
   The following terms are used in this report:
   - Payroll expenses: all expenses related to employee salaries, wages, benefits, and taxes.
   - Salaries: regular payments made to employees for their work.
   - Wages: payments made to contractors for their work.
   - Benefits: additional compensation provided to employees, such as health insurance and retirement plans.
   - Taxes: federal, state, and local taxes paid on behalf of employees.
