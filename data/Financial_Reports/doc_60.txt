
   Financial Restatement Notice

   Introduction:
   * The purpose of this notice is to inform stakeholders of the financial restatement of our company, GreenTech Inc., for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2022.
   * The restatement is a result of an error in the accounting treatment of certain revenue recognition policies.
   * The error was discovered during an internal review of our financial statements by our audit committee, led by Chairman, Jane Smith.

   Background:
   * GreenTech Inc. is a leading provider of renewable energy solutions, with operations in over 10 countries worldwide.
   * Our revenue for the fiscal year 2022 was $10.2 million, with a net income of $1.5 million.
   * The error in revenue recognition resulted in an overstatement of revenue by $500,000, which is approximately 4.9% of our total revenue.
   The following are the key highlights of the restatement:
   * The restatement will result in a reduction of our net income by $200,000, or 13.3% of our originally reported net income.
   * The error did not affect our cash flows or liquidity position.
   * We have taken corrective actions to prevent similar errors in the future, including the implementation of new accounting policies and procedures.

   Restatement Details:
   * The restatement affects the following financial statements:
     + Balance Sheet as of December 31, 2022
     + Income Statement for the year ended December 31, 2022
     + Cash Flow Statement for the year ended December 31, 2022
   * The restatement does not affect our previously reported earnings per share (EPS) of $0.25 per share.
   * The following are the revised financial highlights:
     + Revenue: $9.7 million
     + Net Income: $1.3 million
     + EPS: $0.23 per share

   Impact on Financial Statements:
   * The restatement will result in the following changes to our financial statements:
     + Decrease in revenue by $500,000
     + Decrease in net income by $200,000
     + No change in cash flows
   * The restatement does not affect our compliance with debt covenants or other contractual obligations.
   * We have notified our lenders and other stakeholders of the restatement and do not expect any adverse consequences.

   Conclusion:
   * We apologize for any inconvenience this restatement may have caused and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of financial reporting and transparency.
   * We have taken steps to prevent similar errors in the future and are confident in the accuracy of our revised financial statements.
   * If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact our investor relations department at (555) 123-4567 or [investor.relations@greentechinc.com](mailto:investor.relations@greentechinc.com).
