
   
# Introduction to Financial Restatement Notice

   This financial restatement notice is issued by the management of GreenTech Inc., a leading provider of renewable energy solutions, to inform stakeholders about the restatement of our previously issued financial statements for the fiscal years 2020 and 2021. The restatement is necessitated by the identification of material weaknesses in our internal control over financial reporting, specifically related to the accounting for revenue recognition and lease liabilities.

   
## Background

   On February 10, 2023, our Audit Committee commenced an investigation into certain accounting practices, following the receipt of an anonymous whistleblower complaint. The investigation, conducted with the assistance of external counsel and forensic accountants, revealed that we had incorrectly recognized revenue on certain contracts, failing to properly apply the requirements of Accounting Standards Codification (ASC) 606. Furthermore, we had not accurately accounted for lease liabilities under ASC 842, resulting in an understatement of our operating lease liabilities.

   
The financial impact of these errors is summarized in the following table:

   | Fiscal Year | Incorrect Revenue Recognition | Understated Lease Liabilities |

   |-------------|-------------------------------|--------------------------------|

   | 2020        | $1,250,000                    | $500,000                       |

   | 2021        | $1,800,000                    | $750,000                       |

   
## Restatement Details

   As a result of the investigation and the subsequent restatement, our previously reported net income for the fiscal year 2020 has been reduced by $1,000,000, from $5,000,000 to $4,000,000. For the fiscal year 2021, the net income has been reduced by $1,200,000, from $6,500,000 to $5,300,000. These adjustments reflect the correction of the revenue recognition errors and the proper accounting for lease liabilities.

   
Below are the key restatement adjustments:

   - Reduction in revenue for 2020: $1,250,000

   - Reduction in revenue for 2021: $1,800,000

   - Increase in operating lease liabilities as of December 31, 2020: $500,000

   - Increase in operating lease liabilities as of December 31, 2021: $750,000

   
## Impact on Financial Statements

   The restatement has a significant impact on our financial statements. The corrected financial statements for the fiscal years 2020 and 2021 are presented in the appendix to this notice. The key effects of the restatement on our financial position and results of operations are as follows:

   
1. **Balance Sheet:** The restatement results in an increase in our total liabilities and a corresponding decrease in our retained earnings. As of December 31, 2021, our total liabilities increased by $1,250,000, reflecting the correction of lease liabilities and the impact of the revenue recognition adjustments.

   
2. **Income Statement:** The restatement leads to a reduction in our net income for both fiscal years 2020 and 2021, as previously reported. The corrected net income figures are $4,000,000 for 2020 and $5,300,000 for 2021.

   
## Conclusion

   The management of GreenTech Inc. takes full responsibility for the errors that led to the restatement of our financial statements. We have taken immediate action to remediate the material weaknesses in our internal control over financial reporting and to enhance our accounting practices and policies. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of financial reporting and transparency.

   
In accordance with our governance policies, the Board of Directors and the Audit Committee are overseeing the implementation of these remedial actions. We will continue to monitor our financial reporting processes to ensure compliance with all applicable accounting standards and regulatory requirements.
