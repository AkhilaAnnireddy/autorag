Grievance Redressal Policy

## Introduction
The Grievance Redressal Policy is designed to provide a fair and transparent mechanism for employees to raise and resolve grievances. This policy applies to all employees of NovaTech Inc., a leading technology firm with over 1,000 employees across the globe. Our HR department, led by Ms. Rachel Lee, is committed to ensuring that all grievances are addressed in a timely and professional manner.

## Scope and Applicability
This policy covers all employees, including full-time, part-time, and contract workers. The policy does not apply to third-party vendors or external partners. Employees can raise grievances related to their employment, including but not limited to, workplace harassment, discrimination, and unfair treatment. According to our records, in the year 2022, we received a total of 50 grievances, out of which 30 were resolved within a month.

### Grievance Procedure
To raise a grievance, employees must follow the steps outlined below:
* Step 1: Employee raises the grievance with their immediate supervisor or HR representative within 7 working days of the incident.
* Step 2: The supervisor or HR representative will acknowledge receipt of the grievance and provide a response within 3 working days.
* Step 3: If the grievance is not resolved, the employee can escalate it to the HR department, who will investigate and provide a response within 10 working days.
As stated by our CEO, Mr. David Kim, 'We take all grievances seriously and are committed to providing a fair and transparent process for resolving them.'

## Roles and Responsibilities
The following table outlines the roles and responsibilities of employees and management in the grievance redressal process:
| Role | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| Employee | Raise grievance with supervisor or HR representative |
| Supervisor | Acknowledge and respond to grievance |
| HR Representative | Investigate and respond to grievance |
| HR Department | Provide support and guidance to employees and management |

## Timeline
The grievance redressal process will be completed within 30 working days from the date of receipt of the grievance. The timeline may vary depending on the complexity of the issue and the availability of relevant information. As of January 2023, we have implemented a new grievance tracking system, which has improved our response time by 25%.

## Conclusion
In conclusion, the Grievance Redressal Policy is an essential part of our HR framework, and we are committed to ensuring that all employees have access to a fair and transparent process for resolving grievances. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please do not hesitate to reach out to the HR department. We have a dedicated team, led by Ms. Emily Patel, who will be happy to assist you.

## Grievance Redressal Committee
The Grievance Redressal Committee will be formed to review and resolve grievances that are not resolved through the normal process. The committee will consist of 3 members, including the HR Head, a representative from the management team, and an external expert. The committee will meet quarterly to review grievances and provide recommendations for resolution. As per our policy, the committee will provide a detailed report of their findings and recommendations to the management team within 15 working days of the meeting.
