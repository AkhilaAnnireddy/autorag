As part of our ongoing effort to maintain a professional and respectful work environment, NovaTech Corporation has established a social media usage policy. Firstly, all employees are expected to adhere to our company's code of conduct and ethics policy when using social media. Thirdly, employees are expected to respect the intellectual property rights of others, and to avoid posting or sharing any copyrighted or trademarked materials without permission.
