<h1>Workplace Safety Policy</h1>
   <p>The purpose of this policy is to provide a safe and healthy work environment for all employees of XYZ Corporation. Our goal is to minimize the risk of injury or illness to our employees, visitors, and contractors. The policy applies to all employees, regardless of their position or location.</p>

   <p>Our workplace safety policy is based on the following principles: (1) all incidents can be prevented, (2) all employees have the right to a safe work environment, and (3) management is committed to providing the necessary resources to ensure a safe work environment. The policy will be reviewed and updated annually, or as needed.</p>
