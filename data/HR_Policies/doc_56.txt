
   <h1>Equal Opportunity Policy</h1>
   <p>The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all employees, job applicants, and contractors are treated fairly and without discrimination. Our company, XYZ Corporation, is committed to providing equal opportunities to all individuals, regardless of their race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other characteristic protected by law.</p>
   <h2>Policy Statement</h2>
   <p>Our equal opportunity policy is based on the principles of fairness, respect, and dignity. We believe that every individual has the right to work in an environment that is free from discrimination and harassment. The policy applies to all aspects of employment, including recruitment, selection, training, promotion, and termination. The following table outlines the key principles of our equal opportunity policy:</p>
   <table>
       <tr>
           <th>Principle</th>
           <th>Description</th>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Equal Access</td>
           <td>All employees and job applicants have equal access to employment opportunities, regardless of their background or personal characteristics.</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Non-Discrimination</td>
           <td>We do not discriminate against any individual based on their race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other characteristic protected by law.</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Respect and Dignity</td>
           <td>We treat all employees and job applicants with respect and dignity, and provide a work environment that is free from harassment and bullying.</td>
       </tr>
   </table>

   <h2>Grievance Procedure</h2>
   <p>We have a grievance procedure in place to handle any complaints or concerns that employees may have. The following table outlines the steps involved in our grievance procedure:</p>
   <table>
       <tr>
           <th>Step</th>
           <th>Description</th>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Initial Complaint</td>
           <td>Employees should submit their complaint in writing to their supervisor or HR representative.</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Investigation</td>
           <td>We will investigate all complaints and gather evidence to determine the facts of the case.</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Resolution</td>
           <td>We will work with the employee to resolve the complaint and provide a solution that is fair and reasonable.</td>
       </tr>
   </table>
   <p>Our grievance procedure is overseen by our HR Manager, Jane Smith, who ensures that all complaints are handled fairly and without discrimination.
