
   # Introduction to Leave and Time-Off Policy
   Our company, NovaTech Inc., is committed to providing a healthy work-life balance for all employees. The following leave and time-off policy outlines the various types of leave available to employees and the procedures for requesting time off.
   
   ## Types of Leave
   The company offers the following types of leave:
   * Vacation leave: All regular full-time and part-time employees are eligible for vacation leave. The amount of vacation leave earned per year is as follows:
     + 0-2 years of service: 10 days per year
     + 2-5 years of service: 15 days per year
     + 5-10 years of service: 20 days per year
     + 10+ years of service: 25 days per year
   * Sick leave: All regular full-time and part-time employees are eligible for sick leave. Employees earn 12 days of sick leave per year.
   * Family and medical leave: The company complies with the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) of 1993. Eligible employees are entitled to up to 12 weeks of unpaid leave in a 12-month period for certain family and medical reasons.
   * Bereavement leave: The company provides 5 days of bereavement leave for the death of an immediate family member.
   * Jury duty leave: The company provides paid leave for employees serving on a jury.
   * Military leave: The company provides paid leave for employees serving in the military.
   
   ## Requesting Time Off
   To request time off, employees must submit a request form to their supervisor at least 30 days in advance. The request form must include the following information:
   * Type of leave requested
   * Dates of leave
   * Number of days requested
   * Reason for leave (if applicable)
   The supervisor will review the request and respond to the employee within 5 business days.
   
   ## Approval of Time Off
   The following guidelines apply to the approval of time off:
   * Vacation leave: Approval is based on business needs and employee availability.
   * Sick leave: Approval is automatic, but employees must provide a doctor's note if absent for more than 3 consecutive days.
   * Family and medical leave: Approval is based on eligibility under the FMLA.
   * Bereavement leave: Approval is automatic.
   * Jury duty leave: Approval is automatic.
   * Military leave: Approval is automatic.
   
   ## Pay During Leave
   The following pay guidelines apply during leave:
   * Vacation leave: Employees receive their regular pay during vacation leave.
   * Sick leave: Employees receive their regular pay during sick leave.
   * Family and medical leave: Employees do not receive pay during family and medical leave, but may use accrued paid leave.
   * Bereavement leave: Employees receive their regular pay during bereavement leave.
   * Jury duty leave: Employees receive their regular pay during jury duty leave.
   * Military leave: Employees receive their regular pay during military leave.
   
   ## Accrual of Leave
   The following guidelines apply to the accrual of leave:
   * Vacation leave: Employees accrue vacation leave on a monthly basis.
   * Sick leave: Employees accrue sick leave on a monthly basis.
   The company uses the following accrual rates:
   * Vacation leave: 1.67 days per month (20 days per year)
   * Sick leave: 1 day per month (12 days per year)
   
   ## Carryover of Leave
   The following guidelines apply to the carryover of leave:
   * Vacation leave: Employees may carry over up to 5 days of unused vacation leave to the next year.
   * Sick leave: Employees may carry over all unused sick leave to the next year.
   
   ## Leave Records
   The company maintains accurate and up-to-date records of employee leave. Employees may access their leave records through the company's online portal.
   
   ## Leave Policy Administration
   The HR department is responsible for administering the leave policy. Employees with questions or concerns about the leave policy should contact the HR department.
   
   ## Leave Policy Acknowledgement
   All employees are required to acknowledge that they have read and understood the leave policy. Employees must sign and return the acknowledgement form to the HR department by January 31st of each year.
   
   ## Leave Policy Updates
   The company reserves the right to update the leave policy at any time. Employees will be notified of any changes to the policy through email and the company's online portal.
   
   ## Leave Policy Effective Date
   This leave policy is effective as of February 1, 2022, and supersedes all prior leave policies.
