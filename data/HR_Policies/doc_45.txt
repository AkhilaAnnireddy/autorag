<h1>Promotion and Career Growth Policy</h1>
   <p>The purpose of this policy is to outline the procedures and guidelines for promoting employees within the company, as well as to provide opportunities for career growth and development. This policy applies to all regular full-time and part-time employees.</p>

   <p>To be eligible for promotion, employees must have completed at least 12 months of service with the company and have a performance rating of 4.0 or higher on their most recent performance evaluation. The following table outlines the eligibility criteria:</p>

           <td>12 months</td>
           <td>4.0 or higher</td>
       </tr>
   </table>
