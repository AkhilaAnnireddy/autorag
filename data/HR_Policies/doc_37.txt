
   <h1>Anti-Harassment Policy</h1>
   <p>This policy is effective as of January 1, 2022, and applies to all employees of XYZ Corporation. The purpose of this policy is to provide a workplace that is free from harassment and to ensure that all employees are treated with dignity and respect. Our company is committed to providing a work environment that is free from all forms of harassment, including sexual harassment, and will not tolerate any behavior that constitutes harassment.</p>
   <h2>Definition of Harassment</h2>
   <p>Harassment is defined as any unwelcome conduct, verbal or physical, that creates a hostile or intimidating work environment. This includes, but is not limited to, epithets, slurs, or negative stereotyping, as well as written or graphic material that is posted or distributed in the workplace. The following table provides examples of behaviors that may be considered harassment:</p>
   <table>
       <tr>
           <th>Behavior</th>
           <th>Example</th>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Verbal Harassment</td>
           <td>Using derogatory language or making offensive jokes</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Physical Harassment</td>
           <td>Unwanted touching or physical contact</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
           <td>Visual Harassment</td>
           <td>Displaying offensive images or objects</td>
       </tr>
   </table>
