The Performance Review Policy at GreenTech Inc. is designed to provide a fair and comprehensive evaluation of an employee's performance. This policy applies to all regular full-time and part-time employees. The performance review process is a crucial aspect of our company's approach to managing performance and developing our employees. It provides an opportunity for employees to receive feedback on their performance, set goals for the upcoming review period, and discuss their career development. The performance review process is conducted annually, with the review period commencing on January 1st and concluding on December 31st of each year. The actual review discussions will take place in the first quarter of the following year, with all reviews to be completed by the end of February. 

To ensure that the performance review process is fair and unbiased, all managers are required to undergo training on the performance review policy and procedure prior to conducting reviews with their team members. This training will cover the key aspects of the policy, including how to set SMART goals, provide constructive feedback, and evaluate employee performance. The training will be provided by the HR department and will be conducted on a bi-annual basis, with the next training session scheduled for June 15th, 2024. 

The performance review process involves the following steps: 

* Step 1: Preparation - The manager will prepare for the review by gathering feedback from relevant stakeholders, reviewing the employee's job description, and evaluating the employee's performance against their goals and objectives. 
* Step 2: Review Discussion - The manager will conduct a review discussion with the employee to discuss their performance, provide feedback, and set goals for the upcoming review period. 
* Step 3: Goal Setting - The manager and employee will work together to set SMART goals for the upcoming review period. These goals will be specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound. 
* Step 4: Evaluation - The manager will evaluate the employee's performance against their goals and objectives, using the following evaluation criteria: 

| Criteria | Weightage | 
| --- | --- | 
| Job Knowledge | 30% | 
| Quality of Work | 25% | 
| Communication Skills | 20% | 
| Teamwork | 15% | 
| Adaptability | 10% | 

The evaluation criteria will be used to determine the employee's overall performance rating, which will be based on the following scale: 

| Rating | Description | 
| --- | --- | 
| Exceptional | Consistently exceeds expectations | 
| Exceeds Expectations | Frequently exceeds expectations | 
| Meets Expectations | Consistently meets expectations | 
| Needs Improvement | Occasionally fails to meet expectations | 
| Unsatisfactory | Consistently fails to meet expectations |
