Our company, GreenTech Inc., is committed to maintaining a fair and consistent attendance policy. This policy applies to all employees and is designed to ensure that our business operations run smoothly and efficiently. As stated by our CEO, Emily Chen, 'Regular attendance and punctuality are essential for meeting our business objectives and providing excellent service to our clients.' The attendance policy is based on a points system, where employees accrue points for absences, tardiness, and other attendance-related issues. The points system is as follows: 1 point for each unapproved absence, 0.5 points for each instance of tardiness, and 0.25 points for each early departure. Employees who accumulate 3 or more points within a 30-day period will be subject to disciplinary actions, which may include a verbal warning, written warning, or suspension. It is the responsibility of each employee to familiarize themselves with this policy and to adhere to its guidelines. Our attendance policy is designed to be fair and flexible, allowing employees to balance their work and personal responsibilities. In the event of an unexpected absence, employees must notify their supervisor or HR representative as soon as possible, providing a valid reason for their absence. The company reserves the right to request documentation or evidence to support the reason for absence.
