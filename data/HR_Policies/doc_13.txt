The Travel Policy at NovaTech Inc. is designed to provide a comprehensive guide for employees who travel for business purposes. The policy is effective as of January 1, 2022, and is subject to change with a 30-day notice. 
   * The purpose of this policy is to:
     * Ensure that all travel arrangements are made in a cost-effective and efficient manner
     * Provide a safe and healthy environment for employees while traveling
     * Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
   * Eligibility: All regular full-time and part-time employees are eligible for business travel. 
   * Travel Approval: 
     * All travel must be approved by the employee's supervisor or manager prior to making any travel arrangements
     * The approval process includes submitting a travel request form at least 7 days prior to the travel date
     * The form must include the purpose of the trip, destination, travel dates, and estimated costs
   * Booking Travel Arrangements: 
     * Employees are required to book their travel arrangements through the company's designated travel agency, Atlas Travel
     * The agency can be contacted at (555) 123-4567 or [info@atlastravel.com](mailto:info@atlastravel.com)
     * Employees can also book their arrangements online through the agency's website, using the company's unique login credentials
   * Expenses: 
     * The company will reimburse employees for all reasonable expenses incurred during business travel
     * Expenses include transportation, lodging, meals, and other travel-related expenses
     * Employees are required to submit an expense report within 10 days of returning from their trip
   * Transportation: 
     * The company will reimburse employees for the cost of transportation to and from their destination
     * Employees are encouraged to use public transportation or carpool whenever possible
     * The company has a contract with a car rental agency, NovaRent, which offers discounted rates for employees
   * Lodging: 
     * The company has preferred rates with several hotels, including the Marriott and Hilton
     * Employees are required to stay at one of the preferred hotels whenever possible
     * The company will reimburse employees for the cost of lodging, including taxes and fees
   * Meals: 
     * The company will reimburse employees for the cost of meals incurred during business travel
     * Employees are allowed a daily meal allowance of $50 for breakfast, $75 for lunch, and $100 for dinner
     * Employees are required to submit receipts for all meal expenses
   * Other Travel-Related Expenses: 
     * The company will reimburse employees for other travel-related expenses, including tips, parking fees, and internet charges
     * Employees are required to submit receipts for all expenses
   * Travel Advances: 
     * The company offers travel advances to employees who need to pay for travel-related expenses upfront
     * The advance must be approved by the employee's supervisor or manager prior to the travel date
     * Employees are required to repay the advance within 30 days of returning from their trip
   * Emergency Procedures: 
     * In the event of an emergency, employees should contact the company's emergency hotline at (555) 901-2345
     * The hotline is available 24/7 and is staffed by trained personnel
     * Employees should also contact their supervisor or manager as soon as possible to report the emergency
   * Policy Administration: 
     * The Travel Policy is administered by the HR Department
     * The department is responsible for updating the policy, providing training to employees, and ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and regulations
     * Employees can contact the HR Department with any questions or concerns about the policy at (555) 123-4567 or [hr@novatech.com](mailto:hr@novatech.com)
   * Acknowledgement: 
     * All employees are required to acknowledge that they have read and understood the Travel Policy
     * The acknowledgement form must be signed and returned to the HR Department within 30 days of the policy's effective date
     * The form can be downloaded from the company's intranet or obtained from the HR Department
   * Non-Compliance: 
     * Failure to comply with the Travel Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination
     * The company reserves the right to audit employee travel expenses and may require reimbursement for any non-compliant expenses
     * Employees are responsible for ensuring that all travel arrangements are made in accordance with the policy
   * Reimbursement: 
     * The company will reimburse employees for all approved travel expenses within 30 days of receiving the expense report
     * Employees can expect to receive reimbursement via direct deposit or check
     * The company will notify employees of any discrepancies or issues with their expense report
   * Travel Insurance: 
     * The company offers travel insurance to employees who travel for business purposes
     * The insurance policy covers trip cancellations, interruptions, and delays, as well as medical and dental expenses
     * Employees can purchase the insurance policy through the company's designated travel agency, Atlas Travel
   * Contacts: 
     * For any questions or concerns about the Travel Policy, employees can contact the HR Department at (555) 123-4567 or [hr@novatech.com](mailto:hr@novatech.com)
     * For travel arrangements, employees can contact Atlas Travel at (555) 123-4567 or [info@atlastravel.com](mailto:info@atlastravel.com)
     * For emergency assistance, employees can contact the company's emergency hotline at (555) 901-2345
