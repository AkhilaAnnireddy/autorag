Our intern policy is designed to provide a comprehensive and supportive environment for our interns to learn and grow with our organization. At Smith and Co., we believe that our interns are an integral part of our team and play a crucial role in driving our business forward. Our intern program is open to students and recent graduates from various disciplines, and we receive over 500 applications every year. We have a dedicated team of HR professionals, led by our HR Manager, Emily Chen, who oversee the entire intern program, from recruitment to onboarding and finally to the completion of the internship. The internship period is typically 12 weeks long, and interns are expected to work a minimum of 35 hours per week. We offer a stipend of $600 per week to our interns, which is paid bi-weekly. Our interns also have access to all company facilities, including the gym, cafeteria, and library. We encourage our interns to participate in company-wide events and meetings, and we also organize regular social events specifically for our interns. For example, last year, we organized a summer picnic for our interns, which was attended by over 50 interns and HR staff. Our CEO, Michael Lee, also addresses our interns at the beginning of each internship period, welcoming them to the company and outlining our expectations. We take the safety and well-being of our interns very seriously, and we have a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment and discrimination of any kind. Our interns are also required to sign a confidentiality agreement before starting their internship, which outlines the terms and conditions of their employment. We have a dedicated mentorship program in place, where each intern is assigned a mentor who guides them throughout their internship. Our mentors are experienced professionals who have been with the company for at least 2 years, and they provide valuable guidance and support to our interns. We also conduct regular feedback sessions with our interns, where we solicit their feedback and suggestions on how we can improve our intern program. We have a comprehensive evaluation process in place, which includes regular performance reviews and a final evaluation at the end of the internship period. Our interns are also eligible for a performance-based bonus, which is paid at the end of the internship period. We are proud to say that many of our interns have gone on to become full-time employees with our company, and we believe that our intern program plays a crucial role in identifying and developing future talent. In fact, according to our HR data, over 70% of our interns are offered full-time positions with the company after completing their internship. Our intern program is highly competitive, and we receive applications from top universities and colleges around the world. We have a rigorous selection process in place, which includes a series of interviews and assessments. Our HR team reviews each application carefully, and we select the most talented and motivated individuals to join our team. We believe that our intern program is an excellent opportunity for students and recent graduates to gain valuable work experience and build their skills and knowledge. We are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for our interns, and we strive to make their experience with us a positive and rewarding one. As of 2022, we have expanded our intern program to include remote internships, which has allowed us to attract talent from all over the world. Our remote interns work closely with our team and are expected to participate in all company-wide events and meetings. We use video conferencing tools to facilitate communication and collaboration between our remote interns and the rest of the team. We have found that our remote intern program has been highly successful, and we plan to continue to expand it in the future. In conclusion, our intern policy is designed to provide a comprehensive and supportive environment for our interns to learn and grow with our organization. We believe that our interns are an integral part of our team, and we are committed to providing them with the best possible experience.
