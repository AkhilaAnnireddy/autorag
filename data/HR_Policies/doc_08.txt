
   Employee Benefits Policy

   Our company, GreenTech Inc., is committed to providing a comprehensive benefits package to our employees. The following policies and benefits are effective as of January 1, 2022, and are subject to change.

   Eligibility:
   * All full-time employees are eligible for benefits on the first day of the month following their date of hire.
   * Part-time employees are eligible for benefits after completing 1,000 hours of work in a 12-month period.
   * Eligible dependents include spouses, domestic partners, and children up to age 26.

   Health Insurance:
   * We offer a choice of two health insurance plans: a Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) plan and a Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) plan.
   * The PPO plan has a deductible of $1,000 per individual and $2,500 per family, with a coinsurance rate of 20%.
   * The HMO plan has a deductible of $500 per individual and $1,500 per family, with a coinsurance rate of 10%.
   * Our company pays 80% of the premium for the PPO plan and 90% of the premium for the HMO plan.

   Dental and Vision Insurance:
   * We offer a dental insurance plan with a $50 deductible per individual and $150 per family, with a coinsurance rate of 20%.
   * We offer a vision insurance plan with a $20 copay for eye exams and a $100 allowance for glasses or contacts per year.
   * Our company pays 100% of the premium for the dental and vision insurance plans.

   Life Insurance:
   * We offer a basic life insurance plan with a benefit of $50,000 per employee, paid 100% by the company.
   * Employees may purchase additional life insurance coverage up to $200,000, with the premium paid by the employee.
   * Our company also offers a dependent life insurance plan with a benefit of $10,000 per dependent, paid 100% by the company.

   Disability Insurance:
   * We offer a short-term disability insurance plan with a benefit of 60% of weekly earnings, up to $1,500 per week, for up to 13 weeks.
   * We offer a long-term disability insurance plan with a benefit of 60% of monthly earnings, up to $6,000 per month, after a 90-day waiting period.
   * Our company pays 100% of the premium for the short-term and long-term disability insurance plans.

   Paid Time Off:
   * Employees accrue 10 days of paid vacation per year, with a maximum accrual of 20 days.
   * Employees accrue 10 days of paid sick leave per year, with a maximum accrual of 20 days.
   * Our company observes 10 paid holidays per year, including New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.

   Employee Assistance Program:
   * We offer an employee assistance program (EAP) with up to 5 free counseling sessions per year.
   * The EAP also provides resources for financial planning, legal assistance, and work-life balance.
   * Our company pays 100% of the cost of the EAP.

   Policy Changes:
   * Our company reserves the right to modify or terminate our employee benefits at any time, with or without notice.
   * Changes to our employee benefits will be communicated to employees through email, company intranet, or other means.
   * Employees are responsible for reviewing and understanding our employee benefits policies and procedures.

   Acknowledgement:
   * By accepting employment with GreenTech Inc., employees acknowledge that they have read and understood our employee benefits policies and procedures.
   * Employees are responsible for complying with our employee benefits policies and procedures, and for reporting any changes in their eligibility or benefits to our HR department.
