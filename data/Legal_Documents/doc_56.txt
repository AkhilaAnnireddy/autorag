
   MARKETING PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

   This Marketing Partnership Agreement ('Agreement') is entered into on February 10, 2023 ('Effective Date') by and between GreenTech Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ('GreenTech'), and MarketPro LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of New York with its principal place of business at 456 Broadway, New York, NY 10013 ('MarketPro').

   The parties agree to the following terms and conditions:
   * The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of two (2) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 10.
   * The parties shall work together to develop a comprehensive marketing strategy, which shall include, but not be limited to:
     + Social media campaigns targeting at least 10,000 followers on Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn
     + Email marketing campaigns to at least 50,000 subscribers
     + Sponsorship of at least two (2) industry events per year
   * MarketPro shall provide GreenTech with a detailed report on the performance of the marketing campaigns on a quarterly basis, which shall include, but not be limited to:
     + The number of followers on each social media platform
     + The number of email opens and clicks
     + The number of leads generated from each campaign

   Responsibilities of GreenTech:
   * GreenTech shall provide MarketPro with all necessary information and materials to develop and implement the marketing strategy, including, but not limited to:
     + Product descriptions and images
     + Target audience demographics
     + Brand guidelines
   * GreenTech shall review and approve all marketing materials developed by MarketPro before they are published or distributed.

   Responsibilities of MarketPro:
   * MarketPro shall develop and implement the marketing strategy, which shall include, but not be limited to:
     + Creating and scheduling social media posts
     + Designing and sending email marketing campaigns
     + Coordinating and executing industry events
   * MarketPro shall ensure that all marketing materials developed and implemented under this Agreement comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

   Compensation:
   * MarketPro shall be paid a monthly fee of $10,000 for its services under this Agreement.
   * MarketPro shall also be eligible for a performance-based bonus of up to $20,000 per year, based on the achievement of certain milestones, including, but not limited to:
     + Increasing the number of followers on each social media platform by at least 20% per year
     + Increasing the number of leads generated from each campaign by at least 30% per year

   Confidentiality:
   * The parties agree to keep confidential all information and materials exchanged under this Agreement, including, but not limited to:
     + Business plans and strategies
     + Customer lists and contact information
     + Financial information and projections

   Termination:
   * Either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other party if the other party breaches any of its obligations under this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days.
   * Upon termination, MarketPro shall return all confidential information and materials to GreenTech, and GreenTech shall pay MarketPro for all services rendered prior to termination.

   Governing Law:
   * This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
   * Any disputes arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

   Entire Agreement:
   * This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties.

   Amendments:
   * This Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties.
