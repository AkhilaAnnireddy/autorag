This Lease Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into on this 15th day of March, 2024, by and between GreenTech Properties, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ('Landlord'), and NovaSpire, Inc., a California corporation ('Tenant'). The Landlord agrees to lease to the Tenant, and the Tenant agrees to lease from the Landlord, the office space ('Premises') located at 1234 Main Street, Suite 500, Irvine, California 92614. The Premises consist of approximately 5,000 square feet of office space, as shown on the floor plan attached hereto as Exhibit A. The term of this Lease shall commence on June 1, 2024, and shall continue for a period of sixty (60) months. The monthly rent for the Premises shall be $23,000, payable on the first day of each month. The Tenant shall also pay its proportionate share of the operating expenses, which shall be calculated based on the Tenant's percentage of occupancy of the building. The Landlord shall be responsible for maintaining the common areas of the building, including the lobby, hallways, and restrooms. The Tenant shall be responsible for maintaining the Premises, including all equipment, fixtures, and improvements. The Landlord reserves the right to increase the rent upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the Tenant. The Tenant shall have the option to renew this Lease for an additional thirty-six (36) months, provided that the Tenant gives written notice to the Landlord at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the expiration of the initial term. The Tenant shall comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and ordinances, including the Americans with Disabilities Act. The Landlord and the Tenant agree to indemnify and hold harmless each other from any claims, damages, or expenses arising out of or related to this Lease. The Tenant shall maintain liability insurance with a minimum coverage of $1,000,000, naming the Landlord as an additional insured. The Landlord shall maintain property insurance with a minimum coverage of $5,000,000. The Tenant shall not assign or sublease the Premises without the prior written consent of the Landlord. Any assignment or sublease shall be subject to the terms and conditions of this Lease. The Landlord and the Tenant agree to mediate any disputes arising out of or related to this Lease. The mediation shall be conducted in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The Tenant shall pay a security deposit in the amount of $46,000, which shall be refundable at the end of the Lease term, provided that the Tenant has complied with all terms and conditions of this Lease. The Landlord shall have the right to inspect the Premises at any time during business hours, with at least twenty-four (24) hours' written notice to the Tenant. The Tenant shall not make any alterations or improvements to the Premises without the prior written consent of the Landlord. Any alterations or improvements made by the Tenant shall be at the Tenant's sole expense and shall become the property of the Landlord at the end of the Lease term. The Landlord and the Tenant acknowledge that they have read and understand this Lease and have executed it voluntarily. This Lease constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements, whether written or oral.
