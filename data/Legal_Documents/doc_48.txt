
Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between XYZ Corporation and ABC Limited

Introduction:
The purpose of this MOU is to outline the terms and understanding between XYZ Corporation (hereinafter referred to as 'XYZ') and ABC Limited (hereinafter referred to as 'ABC') for a potential partnership in the development of a new software product. The parties have agreed to the following terms and conditions:

* The partnership will be formed for a period of two years, commencing from the date of signing of this MOU.
* The partnership will be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
* The parties will work together to develop a new software product, with the following key features:
  * Cloud-based infrastructure
  * Artificial intelligence-powered analytics
  * User-friendly interface
* The parties will share the costs and revenues of the partnership, with the following split:
  * XYZ: 60%
  * ABC: 40%

Roles and Responsibilities:
The parties have agreed to the following roles and responsibilities:
* XYZ will be responsible for:
  * Developing the software product
  * Providing technical support
  * Managing the sales and marketing efforts
* ABC will be responsible for:
  * Providing financial support
  * Assisting with sales and marketing efforts
  * Providing industry expertise

Financial Terms:
The parties have agreed to the following financial terms:
* The total investment for the partnership will be $1 million, with the following split:
  * XYZ: $600,000
  * ABC: $400,000
* The parties will share the revenues of the partnership, with the following split:
  * XYZ: 60%
  * ABC: 40%
* The parties will review and revise the financial terms every six months, with the first review scheduled for January 15, 2024.

Intellectual Property:
The parties have agreed to the following terms regarding intellectual property:
* All intellectual property rights related to the software product will be owned by XYZ.
* ABC will have a non-exclusive license to use the software product for its internal purposes.
* The parties will work together to protect the intellectual property rights of the software product.

Dispute Resolution:
The parties have agreed to the following terms regarding dispute resolution:
* Any disputes arising out of or related to this MOU will be resolved through arbitration, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
* The arbitration will be conducted by a single arbitrator, appointed by the parties.
* The arbitrator's decision will be final and binding on the parties.

Termination:
The parties have agreed to the following terms regarding termination:
* This MOU will terminate on December 31, 2025, unless extended by the parties in writing.
* Upon termination, the parties will cooperate to transfer all assets and liabilities related to the partnership.
* The parties will also cooperate to complete any outstanding tasks and obligations.

Governing Law:
This MOU will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.

Entire Agreement:
This MOU constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties.

Amendments:
This MOU may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties.

3. June 30, 2023: Completion of software development
4. July 15, 2023: Launch of software product

The following are the financial projections for the partnership:

* Net Income: $500,000 (Year 1), $1.5 million (Year 2)
