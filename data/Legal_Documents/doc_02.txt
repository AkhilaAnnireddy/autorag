Data Privacy Policy of NovaTech Inc.

Introduction:
* This Data Privacy Policy (the "Policy") is effective as of January 1, 2022, and applies to all personal data collected, used, and disclosed by NovaTech Inc. ("NovaTech" or "we") in the course of our business operations.
* NovaTech is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of personal data and has implemented robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of personal data.

Scope:
* This Policy applies to all NovaTech employees, contractors, and third-party service providers who handle personal data on behalf of NovaTech.
* This Policy also applies to all personal data collected, used, or disclosed by NovaTech, regardless of the medium or format in which it is stored or transmitted.

Data Collection:
* NovaTech collects personal data from various sources, including but not limited to:
  + Employee and contractor data, such as name, address, phone number, email address, and employment history.
  + Customer data, such as name, address, phone number, email address, and purchase history.
  + Website visitor data, such as IP address, browser type, and browsing history.
* NovaTech collects personal data for the following purposes:
  + To provide products and services to customers.
  + To manage employee and contractor relationships.
  + To improve the quality and functionality of our website and services.

Data Use and Disclosure:
* NovaTech uses personal data for the purposes specified in the Data Collection section above.
* NovaTech may disclose personal data to:
  + Third-party service providers who assist us in providing products and services to customers.
  + Law enforcement agencies or regulatory authorities, as required by law.
  + Other NovaTech affiliates or subsidiaries, as necessary to achieve our business objectives.

Data Security:
* NovaTech has implemented robust security measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
* These security measures include:
  + Encryption of personal data in transit and at rest.
  + Access controls, such as passwords and biometric authentication.
  + Regular security audits and penetration testing.
* NovaTech has a dedicated data protection team that monitors and responds to security incidents.

Data Retention:
* NovaTech retains personal data for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes specified in the Data Collection section above.
* The retention period for personal data varies depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.
* For example, employee data is typically retained for a period of 7 years after the employee has left the company, while customer data is typically retained for a period of 5 years after the customer has last interacted with us.

Data Subject Rights:
* Data subjects have the right to access, rectify, and erase their personal data.
* Data subjects also have the right to restrict the processing of their personal data and to object to the processing of their personal data for direct marketing purposes.
* To exercise these rights, data subjects should contact NovaTech's data protection officer at dpo@novatech.com.

Compliance and Governance:
* NovaTech is committed to complying with all applicable data protection laws and regulations.
* NovaTech has established a data governance framework that includes policies, procedures, and standards for the management of personal data.
* The data governance framework is reviewed and updated annually to ensure that it remains effective and aligned with industry best practices.

Contact Us:
* If you have any questions or concerns about this Policy, please contact NovaTech's data protection officer at dpo@novatech.com.
* You can also write to us at NovaTech Inc., 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA 12345.

Changes to this Policy:
* NovaTech reserves the right to modify or update this Policy at any time.
* Any changes to this Policy will be posted on our website and will be effective immediately.
* We encourage you to review this Policy regularly to stay informed about our data protection practices.

Acknowledgement:
* By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Policy.
* If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use our website or services.

Definitions:
* "Personal data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
* "Data subject" means an individual who is the subject of personal data.
* "Processing" means any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means.
