This Service Level Agreement (SLA) is entered into on February 10, 2023, by and between NovaTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ("NovaTech"), and Apex Solutions, a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890 ("Apex"). The purpose of this SLA is to establish the service level expectations for the provision of managed IT services by NovaTech to Apex. The term of this SLA shall commence on February 10, 2023, and continue for a period of two years, unless otherwise terminated in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. 

 The service level metrics that will be used to measure the performance of NovaTech are as follows: 

 * System Uptime: 99.95% 
 * System Response Time: 2 seconds 
 * Network Uptime: 99.99% 
 * Help Desk Response Time: 1 hour 

 The following table outlines the service level targets for each metric: 

| Metric | Target | 
| --- | --- | 
| System Uptime | 99.95% | 
| System Response Time | 2 seconds | 
| Network Uptime | 99.99% | 
| Help Desk Response Time | 1 hour | 

 In the event of a service level failure, NovaTech shall provide Apex with a credit against future invoices as follows: 

 * 1% of monthly recurring charges for System Uptime below 99% 
 * $100 for each hour of System Response Time above 5 seconds 
 * 2% of monthly recurring charges for Network Uptime below 99% 
 * $50 for each hour of Help Desk Response Time above 2 hours
