
   EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

   This Employment Contract ('Contract') is made and entered into on February 10, 2024 ('Effective Date'), by and between Atlas Industries, Inc. ('Employer'), a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001, and Emily J. Miller ('Employee'), an individual residing at 456 Elm Street, Los Angeles, CA 90001.

   * The term of this Contract shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of two (2) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Contract.
   * The Employee's job title shall be Senior Marketing Manager, and she shall report directly to the Chief Marketing Officer.
   * The Employee's duties and responsibilities shall include, but not be limited to:
     + Developing and implementing marketing strategies to increase brand awareness and sales
     + Managing a team of marketing professionals to achieve marketing goals and objectives
     + Collaborating with cross-functional teams to launch new products and services
     + Analyzing market trends and competitor activity to identify opportunities and threats
     + Preparing and managing marketing budgets and forecasts

   COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

   * The Employee's annual base salary shall be $120,000, paid bi-weekly in accordance with the Employer's payroll schedule.
   * The Employee shall be eligible to receive an annual bonus of up to 20% of her base salary, based on her individual performance and the Employer's overall financial performance.
   * The Employee shall be entitled to participate in the Employer's benefit plans, including:
     + Medical, dental, and vision insurance
     + 401(k) retirement plan with a 5% employer match
     + Paid time off (vacation, sick leave, and holidays)
     + Life insurance and disability insurance

   TERMINATION

   * This Contract may be terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party.
   * In the event of termination, the Employee shall be entitled to receive:
     + Payment of any accrued but unpaid base salary and bonus
     + Continuation of benefits for a period of six (6) months
     + Outplacement assistance and career counseling

   CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-COMPETITION

   * The Employee shall maintain the confidentiality of all Employer proprietary and confidential information, including but not limited to trade secrets, business strategies, and customer lists.
   * The Employee shall not engage in any competitive activity during the term of this Contract and for a period of one (1) year after termination, including but not limited to:
     + Working for a competitor
     + Soliciting Employer customers or employees
     + Disclosing Employer confidential information

   DISPUTE RESOLUTION

   * Any disputes arising out of or related to this Contract shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
   * The arbitration shall be conducted by a single arbitrator, and the decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding on both parties.

   ENTIRE AGREEMENT

   * This Contract constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements, whether written or oral.
   * This Contract may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties.
