This Indemnification Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into on this 12th day of March, 2023 ('Effective Date'), by and between GreenTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ('GreenTech'), and BlueSky Ventures, a California limited liability company with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890 ('BlueSky'). The parties agree to indemnify each other against certain claims and liabilities arising out of their business relationship. GreenTech and BlueSky have previously entered into a Supply Agreement dated January 1, 2022, pursuant to which GreenTech has agreed to supply certain components to BlueSky for use in BlueSky's products. As a condition to GreenTech's continued supply of such components, the parties have agreed to enter into this Agreement to allocate the risks of certain claims and liabilities that may arise out of their business relationship. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of five years. Either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other party. In the event of termination, the parties' obligations under this Agreement shall survive for a period of two years after the date of termination. GreenTech shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless BlueSky, its officers, directors, employees, and agents, from and against any and all claims, demands, actions, suits, proceedings, and liabilities arising out of or related to any breach by GreenTech of its obligations under the Supply Agreement. BlueSky shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless GreenTech, its officers, directors, employees, and agents, from and against any and all claims, demands, actions, suits, proceedings, and liabilities arising out of or related to any breach by BlueSky of its obligations under the Supply Agreement. The indemnification obligations of the parties under this Agreement shall be subject to the limitations and exclusions set forth in Section 5 below. In no event shall either party be liable for any consequential, indirect, or punitive damages. The aggregate liability of each party under this Agreement shall not exceed $1,000,000. The parties agree to cooperate fully with each other in the defense of any claim, demand, action, suit, or proceeding arising out of or related to this Agreement. The parties shall maintain accurate and complete records of all transactions and communications related to this Agreement. Either party may request the other party to provide such records, and the other party shall provide such records promptly. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements and understandings, whether written or oral. This Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California. Any dispute arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The parties agree to submit to the jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in the State of California. By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
