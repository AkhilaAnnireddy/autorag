
   AFFILIATE AGREEMENT

   This Affiliate Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into on February 10, 2023 ('Effective Date') by and between GreenTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001 ('Company'), and EcoPartner LLC, a California limited liability company with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Los Angeles, CA 90001 ('Affiliate').

   **Article 1: Definitions**

   | Term | Definition |
   | --- | --- |
   | Affiliate | EcoPartner LLC, a California limited liability company |
   | Company | GreenTech Inc., a Delaware corporation |
   | Effective Date | February 10, 2023 |
   | Term | A period of two (2) years from the Effective Date |

   The Company and the Affiliate agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement. The Company is engaged in the business of developing and marketing eco-friendly products, and the Affiliate is engaged in the business of promoting and selling such products.

   **Article 2: Affiliate Obligations**

   The Affiliate shall:
   - Promote the Company's products through its website, social media, and other marketing channels
   - Provide customer support and service to customers referred by the Affiliate
   - Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and policies

   | Obligation | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Marketing | Promote Company products through various channels |
   | Customer Support | Provide support and service to referred customers |
   | Compliance | Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and policies |

   **Article 3: Company Obligations**

   The Company shall:
   - Provide the Affiliate with marketing materials and support
   - Pay the Affiliate a commission of 10% on all sales generated through the Affiliate's unique referral link
   - Ensure that all products are manufactured and delivered in accordance with applicable laws and regulations

   | Obligation | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Marketing Support | Provide Affiliate with marketing materials and support |
   | Commission | Pay Affiliate 10% commission on sales generated |
   | Product Quality | Ensure products are manufactured and delivered in accordance with laws and regulations |

   The Company's products include the EcoCycle recycling bin, the GreenClean cleaning solution, and the SolarPanel solar panel system. The Affiliate shall promote these products through its marketing channels.

   **Article 4: Payment Terms**

   The Company shall pay the Affiliate a commission of 10% on all sales generated through the Affiliate's unique referral link. The commission shall be paid within thirty (30) days of the end of each calendar month. The following table outlines the payment terms:

   | Month | Sales | Commission |
   | --- | --- | --- |
   | January 2023 | $10,000 | $1,000 |
   | February 2023 | $15,000 | $1,500 |
   | March 2023 | $20,000 | $2,000 |

   The Affiliate shall be responsible for all taxes, fees, and expenses associated with the receipt of the commission.

   **Article 5: Term and Termination**

   The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of two (2) years. Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party.

   The following table outlines the termination fees:

   | Reason for Termination | Fee |
   | --- | --- |
   | Breach of Agreement | $5,000 |
   | Insolvency | $10,000 |
   | Bankruptcy | $15,000 |

   **Article 6: Confidentiality**

   The Affiliate shall maintain the confidentiality of all information and materials provided by the Company, including but not limited to customer lists, sales data, and marketing materials.

   | Confidential Information | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Customer Lists | Lists of customers referred by the Affiliate |
   | Sales Data | Data related to sales generated through the Affiliate's unique referral link |
   | Marketing Materials | Materials provided by the Company for marketing purposes |

   By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement.
