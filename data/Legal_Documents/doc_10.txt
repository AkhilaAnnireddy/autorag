
   Consulting Services Agreement

   This Consulting Services Agreement ('Agreement') is entered into on February 10, 2023, by and between GreenTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ('Client'), and ExpertConsulting LLC, a California limited liability company with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890 ('Consultant').

   * The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of two (2) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 10.
   * The Consultant will provide the following services:
     + Development of a comprehensive marketing strategy
     + Creation of a detailed business plan
     + Provision of ongoing consulting services
   * The Client shall pay the Consultant a monthly fee of $10,000, due on the 15th day of each month, commencing on March 15, 2023.
   * The following are the key performance indicators (KPIs) that will be used to measure the success of the consulting services:
     + Increase in sales revenue by at least 15% within the first year
     + Improvement in customer satisfaction ratings by at least 20% within the first year
     + Reduction in operational costs by at least 10% within the first year

   Scope of Work

   The Consultant agrees to provide the following services:
   * Conduct market research and analysis to identify new business opportunities
   * Develop and implement a comprehensive marketing strategy
   * Provide ongoing consulting services to the Client, including but not limited to:
     + Strategic planning
     + Operational improvement
     + Financial planning
   * The Consultant shall devote a minimum of 20 hours per week to the provision of services under this Agreement.
   * The Client shall provide the Consultant with access to all necessary information, personnel, and equipment to enable the Consultant to perform its obligations under this Agreement.

   Payment Terms

   The Client shall pay the Consultant the following fees:
   * A monthly fee of $10,000, due on the 15th day of each month, commencing on March 15, 2023
   * A one-time fee of $50,000, due on the signing of this Agreement
   * The following expenses shall be reimbursed by the Client:
     + Travel expenses
     + Accommodation expenses
     + Meal expenses
   * The Consultant shall submit invoices to the Client on a monthly basis, and the Client shall pay all invoices within 30 days of receipt.

   Confidentiality

   The Consultant and the Client agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information disclosed under this Agreement, including but not limited to:
   * Business strategies
   * Financial information
   * Customer lists
   * The following are the confidentiality obligations of the parties:
     + The Consultant shall not disclose any confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Client
     + The Client shall not disclose any confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Consultant
   * The confidentiality obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   Intellectual Property

   The Consultant retains all rights, title, and interest in and to any intellectual property developed under this Agreement, including but not limited to:
   * Copyrights
   * Trademarks
   * Patents
   * The Client shall not use any intellectual property developed under this Agreement for any purpose other than as authorized by the Consultant.
   * The following are the intellectual property obligations of the parties:
     + The Consultant shall grant the Client a non-exclusive license to use any intellectual property developed under this Agreement
     + The Client shall not sublicense or assign any intellectual property developed under this Agreement to any third party

   Termination

   This Agreement may be terminated by either party upon written notice to the other, as follows:
   * Upon 30 days' notice, without cause
   * Immediately, in the event of a material breach by the other party
   * The following are the termination obligations of the parties:
     + The Consultant shall return all confidential information and property of the Client upon termination
     + The Client shall pay all outstanding fees and expenses due to the Consultant upon termination
   * The termination of this Agreement shall not affect the rights and obligations of the parties that accrued prior to termination.

   Governing Law

   This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without giving effect to any principles of conflicts of law.
   * The following are the governing law obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree to submit to the jurisdiction of the courts of the State of California
     + The parties agree to waive any right to a trial by jury
   * The governing law obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   Dispute Resolution

   Any disputes arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
   * The following are the dispute resolution obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree to select a neutral arbitrator
     + The parties agree to share the costs of arbitration equally
   * The dispute resolution obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   Entire Agreement

   This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties.
   * The following are the entire agreement obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree that this Agreement is the complete and final expression of the parties' agreement
     + The parties agree that this Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties
   * The entire agreement obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   Amendments

   This Agreement may be amended or modified only in writing signed by both parties.
   * The following are the amendment obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree to negotiate any amendments or modifications in good faith
     + The parties agree to execute any amendments or modifications in a timely manner
   * The amendment obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   Notices

   Any notices required or permitted to be given under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be delivered personally or sent by certified mail, return receipt requested.
   * The following are the notice obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree to provide written notice of any changes to their address or contact information
     + The parties agree to acknowledge receipt of any notices in writing
   * The notice obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   Counterparts

   This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
   * The following are the counterpart obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree to execute this Agreement in multiple counterparts
     + The parties agree to exchange counterparts by facsimile or electronic mail
   * The counterpart obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

   By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
   * The following are the signature obligations of the parties:
     + The parties agree to sign this Agreement in duplicate
     + The parties agree to return a signed copy of this Agreement to the other party
   * The signature obligations of the parties shall survive the termination of this Agreement.
