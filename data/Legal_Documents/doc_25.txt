This Content Licensing Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on February 10, 2023, by and between NovaTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ("NovaTech"), and GreenEarth Media, a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890 ("GreenEarth"). The parties agree to the terms and conditions set forth below. NovaTech is the owner of certain proprietary content, including but not limited to text, images, and videos (the "NovaTech Content"). GreenEarth wishes to obtain a license to use the NovaTech Content in connection with its media publications. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of two years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 10 below. The Effective Date of this Agreement shall be the date of the last signature below. The initial term of the license shall be for two years from the Effective Date, and shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either party gives written notice of termination at least thirty days prior to the end of the then-current term. During the term of the license, GreenEarth shall have the right to use the NovaTech Content in connection with its media publications, including but not limited to print, digital, and online media. GreenEarth shall not use the NovaTech Content for any purpose other than as expressly permitted in this Agreement. GreenEarth shall pay to NovaTech a licensing fee of $50,000 per year, payable in quarterly installments of $12,500 on the first day of each quarter. The first payment shall be due on March 1, 2023. In the event that GreenEarth fails to make any payment when due, NovaTech may, at its option, terminate this Agreement and/or seek any other available remedies. The parties agree that the NovaTech Content is and shall remain the exclusive property of NovaTech. GreenEarth shall not acquire any right, title, or interest in or to the NovaTech Content, except as expressly permitted in this Agreement. GreenEarth shall not modify, alter, or edit the NovaTech Content without the prior written consent of NovaTech. Any such modifications, alterations, or edits shall be the exclusive property of NovaTech. The parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information and materials that are marked as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential. The confidentiality obligations set forth in this Section 7 shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of five years. Either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other party if the other party breaches any of its obligations under this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty days after receipt of written notice. Upon termination of this Agreement, GreenEarth shall immediately cease all use of the NovaTech Content and destroy all copies of the NovaTech Content in its possession. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements and understandings. This Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties. Any notice required or permitted to be given under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed to have been given when delivered personally or sent by certified mail, return receipt requested. The parties agree that this Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without giving effect to any principles of conflicts of law. Any dispute arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitration shall be conducted in the city of San Francisco, California, and the language of the arbitration shall be English. The parties agree that the arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding and that judgment on the award may be entered in any court having jurisdiction. By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
