This Settlement Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into on this 10th day of February, 2023 ('Effective Date'), by and between Acme Corporation ('Acme' or 'Defendant'), a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001, and Jane Doe ('Plaintiff'), an individual residing at 456 Elm Street, Los Angeles, CA 90001. The parties agree to settle and resolve all claims, disputes, and causes of action that were or could have been asserted by Plaintiff against Acme in the lawsuit styled Doe v. Acme Corporation, Case No. 1:22-cv-00123, pending in the United States District Court for the Southern District of New York ('Lawsuit'). The parties have agreed to settle the Lawsuit on the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement. The terms of this Agreement are as follows: Acme agrees to pay to Plaintiff the sum of $525,000.00, which payment shall be made in three installments: $175,000.00 on or before March 15, 2023, $175,000.00 on or before June 15, 2023, and $175,000.00 on or before September 15, 2023. The following are the key points of this settlement: 
    - The payment of $525,000.00 is in full and final settlement of all claims, 
    - The parties agree to mutual releases of all claims, 
    - The parties agree to confidentiality of the terms of this Agreement, 
    - The parties agree to be bound by the terms of this Agreement. 
    As set forth in the table below, the payment schedule is as follows: 
    | Payment Date | Payment Amount |
    | --- | --- |
    | March 15, 2023 | $175,000.00 |
    | June 15, 2023 | $175,000.00 |
    | September 15, 2023 | $175,000.00 |
    In consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained in this Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows: 
    1. Release of Claims: 
       a. Plaintiff, on behalf of herself and her successors, assigns, heirs, and representatives, hereby releases and forever discharges Acme, its officers, directors, employees, agents, and affiliates, from any and all claims, demands, actions, causes of action, and liabilities of any nature whatsoever, whether known or unknown, that Plaintiff ever had, now has, or may have, arising out of or related to the Lawsuit or any of the facts, events, or transactions that were alleged or could have been alleged in the Lawsuit. 
       b. Acme, on behalf of itself and its successors, assigns, heirs, and representatives, hereby releases and forever discharges Plaintiff, her officers, directors, employees, agents, and affiliates, from any and all claims, demands, actions, causes of action, and liabilities of any nature whatsoever, whether known or unknown, that Acme ever had, now has, or may have, arising out of or related to the Lawsuit or any of the facts, events, or transactions that were alleged or could have been alleged in the Lawsuit. 
    2. Confidentiality: 
       a. The parties agree to keep the terms and conditions of this Agreement confidential and not to disclose them to any third party, except as may be required by law or as may be necessary to enforce the terms of this Agreement. 
       b. The parties agree that any breach of the confidentiality provisions of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm and that the parties may seek equitable relief, including injunctive relief, in the event of a breach. 
    As of the Effective Date, the parties have reviewed and understand the terms and conditions of this Agreement, and they agree to be bound by them. The parties acknowledge that they have been represented by counsel in the negotiation and execution of this Agreement and that they have had the opportunity to review and understand the terms and conditions of this Agreement. By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
