
   Research Collaboration Agreement

   This Research Collaboration Agreement ('Agreement') is entered into on February 10, 2023 ('Effective Date'), by and between:
   * NeuroSpark Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001 ('NeuroSpark'),
   * MedTech Innovations Ltd., a company incorporated in England and Wales with its registered office at 456 Oxford Street, London, W1D 1AP, United Kingdom ('MedTech'),
   * University of California, Berkeley, a public research university located at Berkeley, CA 94720 ('UC Berkeley').

   The parties agree to collaborate on a research project titled 'Development of AI-Powered Diagnostic Tools for Neurological Disorders' ('Project'). The Project aims to:
   * Develop and test AI algorithms for diagnosing neurological disorders,
   * Conduct clinical trials to validate the efficacy of the diagnostic tools,
   * Publish research papers and present findings at conferences.

   Roles and Responsibilities:
   * NeuroSpark will provide funding for the Project in the amount of $1,500,000 over a period of 24 months,
   * MedTech will provide access to its proprietary technology and expertise in medical device development,
   * UC Berkeley will provide research personnel, facilities, and equipment.

   Project Timeline:
   1. Month 1-3: Literature review and project planning,
   2. Month 4-6: Development of AI algorithms,
   3. Month 7-12: Clinical trials,
   4. Month 13-18: Data analysis and publication,
   5. Month 19-24: Finalize and present findings.

   Intellectual Property:
   * All intellectual property rights arising from the Project will be jointly owned by the parties,
   * The parties will share all publication rights, with each party having the right to publish research papers and present findings at conferences.

   Confidentiality:
   * The parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information exchanged during the Project,
   * The parties will not disclose any confidential information to third parties without prior written consent.

   Dispute Resolution:
   * Any disputes arising from the Agreement will be resolved through arbitration,
   * The arbitration will be conducted in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

   Governing Law:
   * The Agreement will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York,
   * The parties agree to submit to the jurisdiction of the courts of the State of New York.

   By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
