This Settlement Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on February 10, 2023, by and between GreenTech Inc. ("GreenTech"), a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345, and EcoCycle LLC ("EcoCycle"), a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of California with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890.

The parties have agreed to settle all claims arising out of the dispute regarding the alleged infringement of U.S. Patent No. 9,876,543 (the "Patent") by GreenTech. The parties have engaged in good faith negotiations and have reached a mutually acceptable settlement of the dispute. The terms and conditions of the settlement are set forth below.

The consideration for this Agreement shall be as follows:

* GreenTech shall pay to EcoCycle the sum of $500,000, payable in two installments: $250,000 on the date of this Agreement and $250,000 on June 10, 2023.
* EcoCycle shall grant to GreenTech a non-exclusive license to use the Patent for a period of five years from the date of this Agreement, with an option to renew for an additional two years upon payment of a renewal fee of $50,000.
* GreenTech shall provide to EcoCycle a report detailing its use of the Patent on an annual basis, due on January 15th of each year.
