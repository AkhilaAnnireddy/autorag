This Service Level Agreement (SLA) is entered into on February 10, 2024, by and between NovaTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ("NovaTech"), and Apex Solutions, a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890 ("Apex"). The purpose of this SLA is to define the service level expectations for the provision of managed IT services by NovaTech to Apex. The term of this SLA shall commence on March 1, 2024, and shall continue for a period of two years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms of this SLA. The services to be provided by NovaTech under this SLA include network monitoring, help desk support, and cybersecurity services. The service level targets for these services are as follows: network uptime of 99.99%, help desk response time of 15 minutes, and resolution time of 2 hours for critical issues. The service level targets are measured on a monthly basis, and the measurement period shall be from the 1st day of each month to the last day of each month. The following table sets out the service level targets and the corresponding penalties for failure to meet these targets: 

| Service | Target | Penalty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Network Uptime | 99.99% | $1,000 per hour of downtime |
| Help Desk Response Time | 15 minutes | $500 per incident |
| Resolution Time for Critical Issues | 2 hours | $1,500 per incident |

The penalties shall be paid by NovaTech to Apex within 30 days of the end of each measurement period. The following are the key performance indicators (KPIs) that shall be used to measure the performance of NovaTech under this SLA: First Response Time (FRT), Mean Time To Resolve (MTTR), and Customer Satisfaction (CSAT). The KPIs shall be measured on a monthly basis, and the measurement period shall be from the 1st day of each month to the last day of each month. The following are the KPI targets: FRT of 15 minutes, MTTR of 2 hours, and CSAT of 90%. The KPI targets are as follows: 

* FRT: The time taken by NovaTech to respond to an incident reported by Apex. The target FRT is 15 minutes.
* MTTR: The time taken by NovaTech to resolve an incident reported by Apex. The target MTTR is 2 hours.
* CSAT: The satisfaction of Apex with the services provided by NovaTech. The target CSAT is 90%. 

The KPIs shall be reported to Apex on a monthly basis, within 10 days of the end of each measurement period. The report shall include the following information: the number of incidents reported, the number of incidents resolved, the FRT, MTTR, and CSAT. The report shall be in the following format: 

| Month | Incidents Reported | Incidents Resolved | FRT | MTTR | CSAT |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| January | 100 | 90 | 10 minutes | 1 hour | 85% |
| February | 120 | 100 | 12 minutes | 1.5 hours | 88% |

Any changes to this SLA shall be made in writing and signed by both parties.
