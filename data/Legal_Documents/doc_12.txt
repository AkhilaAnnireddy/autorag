This Shareholder Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into on February 10, 2023, by and among GreenTech Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), and its shareholders, John Lee, Michael Chen, and Emily Patel (collectively, the "Shareholders"). The Company is engaged in the development and commercialization of renewable energy technologies. The Shareholders collectively own 100% of the outstanding shares of the Company's common stock, with John Lee owning 40%, Michael Chen owning 30%, and Emily Patel owning 30%. The purpose of this Agreement is to provide a framework for the governance and operation of the Company, including the roles and responsibilities of the Shareholders and the management of the Company. The Shareholders agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement, which shall supersede all prior agreements and understandings among the Shareholders with respect to the subject matter hereof. The Company's Board of Directors (the "Board") shall consist of three members, with John Lee, Michael Chen, and Emily Patel each serving as a director. The Board shall be responsible for overseeing the management of the Company and making major strategic decisions. The Shareholders have agreed to the following capitalization table:

   The Company has adopted a dividend policy, which provides that the Company shall distribute 20% of its annual net income to the Shareholders. The dividend shall be paid quarterly, within 45 days after the end of each quarter. The Shareholders have agreed to the following restrictions on transfer of shares: (i) no Shareholder may transfer any shares of the Company without the prior written consent of the Board; and (ii) any transfer of shares shall be subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement. The Company has established a confidentiality policy, which provides that all confidential information shall be kept confidential and shall not be disclosed to any third party without the prior written consent of the Board. The Shareholders have agreed to the following dispute resolution procedure: any dispute arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitration shall be conducted by a single arbitrator, who shall be selected by the parties. The arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding on the parties. The Company has adopted a policy of indemnifying its officers and directors against any liability or expense incurred in connection with any proceeding brought against them by reason of their serving as officers or directors of the Company. The policy provides that the Company shall indemnify its officers and directors to the fullest extent permitted by law. The Shareholders have agreed to the following termination provisions: this Agreement shall terminate upon the occurrence of any of the following events: (i) the sale of all or substantially all of the assets of the Company; (ii) the merger or consolidation of the Company with or into another corporation; or (iii) the dissolution or liquidation of the Company. Upon termination of this Agreement, the Shareholders shall return all confidential information to the Company and shall destroy any copies thereof. The Company has established a record date for the purpose of determining the Shareholders entitled to notice of, and to vote at, any meeting of Shareholders.
