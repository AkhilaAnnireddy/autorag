The effective date of this Agreement shall be the date of the last signature below. By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the effective date and continue for a period of two (2) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 9. The Agreement sets forth the terms and conditions under which GreenTech will provide Customer with access to its proprietary software platform (the 'Platform') and related services (the 'Services'). The Platform is a cloud-based software solution designed to help businesses manage their customer data more effectively. The Services include, but are not limited to, data storage, data analytics, and customer support. The Customer shall pay GreenTech an annual fee of $100,000 for the use of the Platform and Services, payable in quarterly installments of $25,000 each. The first payment shall be due on March 15, 2023, and subsequent payments shall be due on June 15, 2023, September 15, 2023, and December 15, 2023. In addition to the annual fee, the Customer shall also pay GreenTech a one-time implementation fee of $20,000, which shall be due upon execution of this Agreement. The implementation fee covers the cost of configuring the Platform to meet the Customer's specific needs and providing training to the Customer's personnel. The following are the key features of the Platform: 
   - Data Storage: The Platform provides secure and scalable data storage solutions to help businesses manage their customer data.
   - Data Analytics: The Platform includes advanced data analytics tools to help businesses gain insights into their customer behavior and preferences.
   - Customer Support: The Platform provides 24/7 customer support to help businesses resolve any issues they may encounter.
   The following are the benefits of using the Platform: 
   1. Improved customer engagement: The Platform helps businesses to better understand their customers and tailor their marketing efforts accordingly.
   2. Increased efficiency: The Platform automates many routine tasks, freeing up staff to focus on higher-value activities.
   3. Enhanced security: The Platform provides robust security features to protect customer data from unauthorized access.
   The following table sets out the Service Level Agreement (SLA) for the Platform:
