
   Data Processing Agreement

   This Data Processing Agreement ('Agreement') is entered into on February 10, 2023 ('Effective Date') by and between:

   * NovaTech Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Wilmington, DE 19801 ('Data Controller')
   * ProxiData LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of California, with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, San Francisco, CA 94105 ('Data Processor')

   Recitals:

   * The Data Controller is engaged in the business of providing software solutions to its clients.
   * The Data Processor is engaged in the business of providing data processing services to its clients.
   * The Data Controller wishes to engage the Data Processor to provide data processing services in connection with the Data Controller's business.

   1. Definitions

   For the purposes of this Agreement, the following definitions shall apply:
   * 'Personal Data' means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person ('Data Subject').
   * 'Processing' means any operation or set of operations which is performed on Personal Data or on sets of Personal Data, whether or not by automated means.
   * 'Data Protection Laws' means all applicable laws and regulations relating to the protection of Personal Data, including the General Data Protection Regulation ('GDPR') and the California Consumer Privacy Act ('CCPA').

   2. Scope of Processing

   The Data Processor shall provide data processing services to the Data Controller as follows:
   * The Data Processor shall process Personal Data on behalf of the Data Controller for the purpose of providing software solutions to the Data Controller's clients.
   * The Data Processor shall process the following types of Personal Data:
     + Name
     + Address
     + Email address
     + Phone number
   * The Data Processor shall process Personal Data for the following purposes:
     + To provide software solutions to the Data Controller's clients
     + To respond to inquiries from the Data Controller's clients
     + To improve the Data Controller's software solutions

   3. Obligations of the Data Processor

   The Data Processor shall:
   * Process Personal Data only on the documented instructions of the Data Controller.
   * Ensure that all personnel authorized to process Personal Data have committed to confidentiality.
   * Implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Personal Data against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
   * Notify the Data Controller without undue delay in the event of a Personal Data breach.

   4. Obligations of the Data Controller

   The Data Controller shall:
   * Provide the Data Processor with all necessary information and documentation to enable the Data Processor to perform its obligations under this Agreement.
   * Ensure that it has obtained all necessary consents and permissions from Data Subjects to allow the Data Processor to process Personal Data.
   * Notify the Data Processor of any changes to the Personal Data or the purposes for which it is processed.

   5. Security Measures

   The Data Processor shall implement and maintain the following security measures to protect Personal Data:
   * Encryption of Personal Data in transit and at rest
   * Access controls, including multi-factor authentication
   * Regular security audits and penetration testing
   * Incident response plan in the event of a Personal Data breach

   6. Data Subject Rights

   The Data Processor shall:
   * Respond to requests from Data Subjects to exercise their rights under the Data Protection Laws.
   * Provide the Data Controller with all necessary information and assistance to enable the Data Controller to respond to requests from Data Subjects.

   7. Term and Termination

   This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in effect until terminated by either party with thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party.
   * Upon termination of this Agreement, the Data Processor shall:
     + Return all Personal Data to the Data Controller.
     + Delete all Personal Data from its systems.
   * The Data Processor shall provide the Data Controller with a certificate of destruction of Personal Data upon request.

   8. Liability and Indemnification

   The Data Processor shall be liable for any damages or losses suffered by the Data Controller as a result of the Data Processor's breach of this Agreement.
   * The Data Processor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Data Controller against any claims, demands, or actions brought by a third party against the Data Controller as a result of the Data Processor's breach of this Agreement.

   9. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

   This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware.
   * Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

   10. Entire Agreement

   This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous agreements or understandings.
   * This Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties.
