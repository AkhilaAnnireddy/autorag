
   Data Privacy Policy of NovaTech Inc.
   
   Introduction:
   * This Data Privacy Policy was last updated on February 27, 2023.
   * It is applicable to all users of NovaTech Inc.'s services, including but not limited to, our website, mobile applications, and software.
   * The policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.
   
   1. Data Collection:
   * We collect the following types of personal data:
     * Name
     * Email address
     * Phone number
     * IP address
     * Location data
   * We collect data through various means, including:
     * User registration
     * Cookies
     * Web beacons
     * Social media platforms
   * Our data collection methods comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
   
   2. Data Usage:
   * We use your personal data for the following purposes:
     * Providing our services to you
     * Improving our services
     * Marketing and advertising
     * Complying with legal requirements
   * We may share your data with third-party service providers, such as:
     * Cloud storage providers
     * Payment processors
     * Marketing agencies
   * We ensure that all third-party service providers comply with our data protection policies.
   
   3. Data Protection:
   * We implement the following security measures to protect your data:
     * Encryption
     * Firewalls
     * Access controls
     * Regular security audits
   * We have a Data Protection Officer (DPO) who is responsible for overseeing our data protection practices.
   * Our DPO can be contacted at dpo@novatech.com.
   
   4. Data Retention:
   * We retain your personal data for the following periods:
     * 2 years for marketing purposes
     * 5 years for financial transactions
     * 10 years for legal compliance
   * We may retain your data for longer periods if required by law or if necessary for the protection of our rights.
   
   5. User Rights:
   * You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
     * Right to access
     * Right to rectification
     * Right to erasure
     * Right to restriction of processing
     * Right to data portability
     * Right to object
   * You can exercise these rights by contacting our DPO at dpo@novatech.com.
   
   6. Changes to this Policy:
   * We may update this policy from time to time.
   * We will notify you of any changes by posting an updated version on our website.
   * Your continued use of our services after the update will be deemed as acceptance of the new policy.
   
   7. Contact Us:
   * If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact our DPO at dpo@novatech.com.
   * You can also reach us by mail at NovaTech Inc., 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345.
   
   8. Governing Law:
   * This policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
   * Any disputes arising out of or related to this policy will be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
   
   9. Definitions:
   * Personal data: any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
   * Processing: any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means.
   * Data controller: the natural or legal person who determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.
