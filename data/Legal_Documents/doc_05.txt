This Non-Disclosure Agreement ('Agreement') is entered into on February 10, 2023, by and between NovaTech Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Wilmington, DE 19801 ('NovaTech'), and Eclipse Solutions, a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, San Francisco, CA 94105 ('Eclipse'). The parties agree that they wish to exchange certain confidential and proprietary information ('Confidential Information') for the purpose of exploring a potential business relationship. In consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the date first above written and shall continue for a period of two years. During this term, either party may disclose Confidential Information to the other party. Confidential Information shall include all information, whether written or oral, that is disclosed by one party to the other party, including but not limited to trade secrets, technical data, product information, business strategies, and software code. The receiving party agrees to hold the Confidential Information in confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party. The receiving party shall use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential business relationship between the parties and shall not use it for any other purpose. The receiving party shall take reasonable precautions to protect the Confidential Information, including but not limited to storing it in a secure location and restricting access to it to those employees who have a need to know. The disclosing party shall be entitled to injunctive relief to enforce the provisions of this Agreement, in addition to any other remedies that it may have. The parties agree that the Confidential Information is and shall remain the property of the disclosing party, and that the receiving party shall not acquire any rights or interests in it. The parties further agree that they shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding the exchange of Confidential Information, including but not limited to the Defend Trade Secrets Act of 2016. On January 1, 2022, NovaTech filed a patent application with the United States Patent and Trademark Office for a new software product, and the details of this application are considered Confidential Information under this Agreement. As of the date of this Agreement, Eclipse has 50 employees and annual revenues of $10 million. NovaTech has 200 employees and annual revenues of $50 million. The parties agree to negotiate in good faith to reach a definitive agreement regarding a potential business relationship within 90 days of the date of this Agreement. If the parties are unable to reach a definitive agreement within this timeframe, either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other party. In the event of termination, the receiving party shall promptly return all Confidential Information to the disclosing party and shall destroy any copies of it that it has made. The parties agree that this Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without giving effect to any choice or conflict of law provision or rule. Any disputes arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The parties agree that the arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding upon them. By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read and understand this Agreement and shall comply with its terms. This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
