
   FREELANCE CONTRACT

   This Freelance Contract (the "Contract") is made and entered into on February 10, 2024 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Emily J. Lee (the "Client") with a principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001, and David M. Kim (the "Freelancer") with a principal place of business at 456 Broadway, Los Angeles, CA 90013.

   Table 1: Project Details
   | Project Name | Project Description | Duration | Rate |
   | --- | --- | --- | --- |
   | Marketing Campaign | Develop a comprehensive marketing campaign for the Client's new product launch | 6 weeks | $1,500/week |
   | Website Design | Design and develop a new website for the Client | 8 weeks | $2,000/week |
   | Content Creation | Create high-quality content for the Client's social media platforms | 12 weeks | $1,000/week |

   The Freelancer agrees to provide the services outlined in Table 1 (the "Services"). The Client agrees to pay the Freelancer the rates outlined in Table 1 for the Services provided. The Freelancer will invoice the Client on a bi-weekly basis, and the Client will pay all invoices within 30 days of receipt.

   Table 2: Payment Terms
   | Payment Method | Payment Schedule | Late Fee |
   | --- | --- | --- |
   | Bank Transfer | Bi-weekly | 2% per month |
   | Check | Monthly | 5% per month |
   | Credit Card | Weekly | 1% per month |

   The Freelancer will be responsible for all equipment, software, and other materials necessary to perform the Services. The Client will provide the Freelancer with access to all necessary information, materials, and personnel to perform the Services.

   Table 3: Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure
   | Confidential Information | Non-Disclosure Obligations |
   | --- | --- |
   | Client's trade secrets | The Freelancer will not disclose any confidential information to any third party |
   | Client's business strategies | The Freelancer will not use any confidential information for any purpose other than to perform the Services |
   | Client's customer lists | The Freelancer will return all confidential information to the Client upon completion of the Services |

   The term of this Contract will commence on the Effective Date and will continue for a period of 12 months. Either party may terminate this Contract upon 30 days' written notice to the other party.

   Table 4: Termination and Notice
   | Termination Notice | Termination Fee |
   | --- | --- |
   | 30 days' written notice | $5,000 |
   | 60 days' written notice | $10,000 |
   | 90 days' written notice | $15,000 |

   By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Contract.

   Signed for and on behalf of the Client:

   Emily J. Lee
   Date: February 10, 2024

   Signed for and on behalf of the Freelancer:

   David M. Kim
