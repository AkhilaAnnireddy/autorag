This Software Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on February 10, 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Novum Technologies, Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, Anytown, CA 12345 ("Novum"), and Apex Business Solutions, LLC, a California limited liability company with its principal place of business at 456 Elm Street, Othertown, CA 67890 ("Apex"). Novum is a leading provider of software solutions for the financial services industry, and Apex is a consulting firm that specializes in implementing technology solutions for its clients. The parties have agreed that Novum will provide certain software services to Apex, as more fully described below. The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of three years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. During the term of this Agreement, Novum shall provide Apex with access to its proprietary software platform, known as the Novum Financial Platform (the "Platform"). The Platform is a web-based application that provides a range of tools and features for managing financial transactions, including account reconciliation, payment processing, and financial reporting. Apex shall be responsible for ensuring that its authorized users comply with all applicable laws and regulations, as well as the terms and conditions of this Agreement. The fees for the services provided under this Agreement shall be as set forth in Exhibit A, which is attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference. The total fees for the first year of the term shall be $250,000, payable in quarterly installments of $62,500. The fees for the second and third years of the term shall be $275,000 and $300,000, respectively, also payable in quarterly installments. In the event that Apex fails to make any payment when due, Novum may, at its option, charge interest on the overdue amount at a rate of 1.5% per month, or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less. The parties have agreed that the Platform shall be hosted on servers located at Novum's data center in Chicago, Illinois, and that Novum shall be responsible for maintaining the security and integrity of the Platform. Novum shall also provide Apex with technical support and maintenance services, as more fully described in Exhibit B, which is attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference. The support and maintenance services shall be provided during normal business hours, which are defined as 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Central Time, Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. In the event of a system outage or other critical issue, Novum shall provide 24/7 support to Apex. Novum represents and warrants that the Platform shall perform in accordance with the specifications set forth in Exhibit C, which is attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference. If the Platform fails to perform in accordance with the specifications, Novum shall, at its option, either repair or replace the Platform, or provide a refund of the fees paid by Apex for the affected period. Apex represents and warrants that it shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act and the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard. The parties have agreed that this Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state of California, without giving effect to any principles of conflicts of law. Any disputes arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitrator shall be a neutral third-party expert in the field of software licensing and technology law. The arbitration shall be conducted in Los Angeles, California, and the parties shall share the costs of the arbitration equally. In the event that either party fails to comply with the terms of this Agreement, the other party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the non-compliant party. Upon termination, Novum shall cease providing services to Apex, and Apex shall cease using the Platform. Apex shall also return all Novum materials, including documentation and software, to Novum within 10 days of termination. The parties have agreed that this Agreement constitutes the entire understanding of the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof, and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties. This Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties. By executing this Agreement, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
