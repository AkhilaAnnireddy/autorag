
   RESEARCH COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

   This Research Collaboration Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on February 10, 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

   * NeuroSpark Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001 ("NeuroSpark")
   * BioMedix Ltd., a company incorporated in England and Wales with its registered office at 456 Oxford Street, London W1D 1AN, United Kingdom ("BioMedix")
   * Dr. Rachel Kim, Professor of Neuroscience at Harvard University, with an address at 789 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA 02138 ("Dr. Kim")

   The parties agree to collaborate on a research project titled "Development of Novel Therapies for Neurodegenerative Diseases" (the "Project"). The Project will be led by Dr. Kim and will involve the following key objectives:
   * To identify and validate novel therapeutic targets for neurodegenerative diseases
   * To develop and test new compounds for the treatment of neurodegenerative diseases
   * To conduct clinical trials to evaluate the safety and efficacy of new therapies

   The term of this Agreement will commence on the Effective Date and will continue for a period of three (3) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 10. The parties will meet quarterly to review progress and discuss future plans.

   RESPONSIBILITIES

   The responsibilities of the parties are as follows:
   * NeuroSpark will provide funding for the Project in the amount of $1,500,000 per year for three years, for a total of $4,500,000
   * BioMedix will provide access to its proprietary technology and expertise in medicinal chemistry
   * Dr. Kim will oversee the scientific aspects of the Project and ensure that the research is conducted in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations

   INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

   The parties agree that all intellectual property rights arising from the Project will be owned jointly by NeuroSpark and BioMedix. The parties will share all data and results generated during the Project, and will cooperate to prepare and file patent applications.

   CONFIDENTIALITY

   The parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information disclosed during the Project, and to use such information only for the purposes of the Project. The parties will ensure that all employees and contractors involved in the Project have signed confidentiality agreements.

   TERM AND TERMINATION

   The term of this Agreement may be terminated by either party upon written notice to the other party. Upon termination, the parties will cooperate to complete any ongoing work and to transfer all data and materials to the other party.

   DISPUTE RESOLUTION

   Any disputes arising from this Agreement will be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The arbitration will be conducted by a single arbitrator, and the decision of the arbitrator will be final and binding.

   GOVERNING LAW

   This Agreement will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York. The parties agree to submit to the jurisdiction of the courts of the State of New York.

   ENTIRE AGREEMENT

   This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties.
