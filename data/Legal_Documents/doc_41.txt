PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
This Partnership Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into on February 10, 2023 ('Effective Date'), by and between Jane Smith ('Partner A') and John Doe ('Partner B'), collectively referred to as the 'Partners'.

Table 1: Partnership Overview
| Category | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Partnership Name | Smith & Doe Partnership |
| Business Address | 123 Main St, Anytown, USA |
| Partnership Term | 5 years from Effective Date |
| Initial Capital Contribution | $100,000 |

The Partners agree to share profits and losses as set forth in Table 2. The profit-sharing ratio will be 60% for Partner A and 40% for Partner B.

Table 2: Profit-Sharing Ratio
| Partner | Profit-Sharing Ratio |
| --- | --- |
| Partner A | 60% |
| Partner B | 40% |

The Partners will meet quarterly to review the Partnership's financial performance. The first meeting will be held on March 15, 2023, at 10:00 AM EST.

Table 3: Quarterly Meeting Schedule
| Meeting Date | Time |
| --- | --- |
| March 15, 2023 | 10:00 AM EST |
| June 15, 2023 | 10:00 AM EST |
| September 15, 2023 | 10:00 AM EST |
| December 15, 2023 | 10:00 AM EST |

Partner A will be responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of the Partnership, while Partner B will oversee the financial aspects. The Partners will jointly make decisions regarding major investments and strategic direction.

Table 4: Management Responsibilities
| Partner | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Partner A | Day-to-Day Operations |
| Partner B | Financial Management |

The Partnership will maintain a minimum cash reserve of $20,000 to cover unexpected expenses. The Partners will review and update the budget quarterly.

Table 5: Budget Overview
| Category | Budgeted Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Initial Capital Contribution | $100,000 |
| Quarterly Expenses | $10,000 |

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Partners and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the Partners. The Agreement may be amended only in writing signed by both Partners.

Table 6: Amendment and Termination
